'90s-Inspired Halloween Costume Ideas - Best Halloween Costumes 2020

 Posted on March 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  618 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Dionne

Getty Images

Everything we hold dear about the past comes back to Clueless, from the infamous quotes and Valley Girl verbiage to the stylish outfits and bold makeup. Lupita Nyong'o did Dionne justice in 2018.

Buttercup

Getty Images

The Powerpuff Girls takes us way back to eating cereal with early morning cartoons on the weekends. The animated superhero series features three superstar girls, with the toughest and edgiest of the trio being Buttercup.

Pink Power Ranger

Getty Images

Claudia Schiffer gave us endless inspiration for Halloween when she showed up to a party in 2017 dressed like the Pink Power Ranger, complete with neon-pink eye shadow and a matching hair bow.

Iconic Britney Spears

Getty Images

You can't go wrong with a little Britney; the singer inspired much of our youth. Dress as the pop idol by wearing a baby-pink tee while popping bubble gum, or go as her onstage goddess in a sparkling bra and sheer rhinestone pants.

Cruella de Vil

©Buena Vista Pictures/Courtesy Everett Collection

We didn't know it was pure fashion when we were younger, but now Cruella de Vil is recognized as a style icon. Featured in 101 Dalmatians, Glenn Close proves that all you really need for this look is your favorite leopard-print coat and a lot of hairspray.

Carrie Bradshaw

©New Line Cinema/Courtesy Everett Collection

What would the '90s have been without the narrations and closet of NYC lover and shopaholic Carrie Bradshaw? The character played by Sarah Jessica Parker will forever go down as the best of all time. Find a frothy tutu to channel this iconic ballerina-inspired look.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Céline Dion

Getty Images

Our hearts will always follow Céline Marie Claudette Dion. The iconic superstar has given us hours of dance material, resulting in plenty of novice music videos filmed from the comfort of our pink bedroom. A fan of sheer dresses, Dion always stuns. All you'll need to achieve this look is a beach cover-up and platform heels.

Salt-N-Pepa

beyonce //Instagram

Beyoncé, Blue Ivy, and Tina Lawson showed up for Halloween one year as American hip-hop girl group Salt-N-Pepa. Beside providing us with some of our favorite songs of all time, the hip-hop group has stocked us with super-'90s ensembles to copy.

Barbie

Getty Images

When Rita Ora dressed up as Barbie in 2015, everyone applauded. That's how iconic the doll still is today. Pink, pink, plus more pink is the winning formula for this Halloween costume. Complete the look with a body-con dress and voluminous waves.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle

Instagram

Rihanna rocked a Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle costume in 2014, and there's no way her "hero in a half shell" getup doesn't take you right back to when you knew every word of that turtle-power theme song.

Wednesday Addams

Everett Collection

This Halloween costume goes without explaining. Wednesday Addams was every creative girl's favorite rebel in the '90s. Just braid your hair and put on an unimpressed expression for this one.

Princess Diana

Getty Images

We always revisit Princess Diana's most memorable looks, and one of our go-tos is her off-duty street style. Get out your neon bike shorts, dad sneakers, and a sweatshirt to re-create this errand-running outfit.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Rachel Green

Getty Images

Rachel Green, played by Jennifer Aniston, stole our hearts on Friends, a show many fans are begging to come back. Rachel often wore relatable, youthful looks that you probably already own, like this layered Cookie Monster ensemble.
