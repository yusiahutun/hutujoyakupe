Remember when Harry Styles used to strut around in YSL Chelsea boots all the time both on and off the stage? I’m talking peak long-hair Harry when his daily uniform was literally just ripped skinny jeans, a t-shirt or button-down top, and rockstar-looking Chelsea boots. I’ll always cherish that era (2014-2016 Harry), but this shoe style is more than just a One Direction sartorial phase. Chelsea boots are one of the most classic closet staples. Our fashion king influenced me to go through my own Chelsea boots phase back then, and I’m more than ready to revisit that chapter.

Now not everyone has thousands of dollars to drop on YSL shoes, but luckily, you don’t have to. AllSaints, a brand I used to be obsessed with during my Harry Chelsea boots era, has still got it. The brand—which is most widely known for its celeb-loved leather jackets—has elevated the Chelsea boot to look more on par with today’s fashion scene. Think chunky soles, a Western flair, and knee-high styles.

AllSaints’ offerings are super versatile and high-quality, and they also just so happen to be marked down. Through August 29, score Chelsea boots and other clothing, accessories, and shoes for up to 60 percent off. AllSaints’ regular-priced items are usually treats to save up for, but these deals are simply too good to pass up, especially with autumn creeping up.
