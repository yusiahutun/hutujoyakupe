Sam Claflin bio

Samuel George Claflin, an English actor, was born on 27 June 1986 to Mark and Sue Claflin. He has three siblings, Benjamin, Daniel, and Joseph, also an actor. He grew up in Norwich, Norfolk.

As a child, Sam had an interest in football. He played throughout his childhood until he broke his ankle, a severe injury that he felt would deny him the chance to play professionally. After persuasion from his parents and teacher, whom he had impressed during his high school play, he pursued acting.

Sam Claflin's acting career

Sam Claflin graduated in 2009 from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art and began his TV acting career in 2011. His first film role was Philip Swift in Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides (2011).
