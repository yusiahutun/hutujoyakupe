KFC launches new Tower Burgers that are stuffed with hash browns

 Posted on May 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  329 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Us Brits love tucking into a burger now and then.

And there's good news for food fans - as there are now some tempting new flavours to try out.

KFC has launched new and improved flavours – from the BBQ Fillet Tower Burger to the Zinger Supercharger Tower Burger and the Original Fillet Tower Burger.

You can snap them up for £6.99 or as part of a meal offer for £10.49.

Every burger is loaded with signature crispy hash browns, topped with a 100% Original Chicken Fillet, cheese and a seeded bun.

The fast food restaurant has kicked off September with a bang by introducing its new fully-loaded updated Tower Burger range.

KFC said the revamped range promises a mega mouth-watering adventure that’s big on satisfaction and full on flavour.

Whether you’re a fan of a Zinger kick fired up with Supercharger sauce, keeping it classic with the original Fillet, or prefer the smoky richness of BBQ – there’s a Tower Burger for everyone.

If that doesn’t get your mouth watering, fans can go all out by making it a meal, adding a side of regular fries and a drink.

The new Tower Burgers were launched on September 4, and will be available via KFC restaurants and via the KFC App for collection.
