About

American Actress Patricia Neal was born Patricia Louise Neal on 20th January, 1926 in Packard, Kentucky and passed away on 8th Aug 2010 Edgartown, Massachusetts aged 84. She is most remembered for The Day the Earth Stood Still. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

American Actor Paul Newman was born Paul Leonard Newman on 26th January, 1925 in Shaker Heights, Ohio, United States and passed away on 26th Sep 2008 Westport, Connecticut, United States aged 83. He is most remembered for Brick Pollitt In 'Cat On A Hot Tin Roof' (1958), Rocky Graziano In 'Somebody Up There Likes Me' (1956), Butch Cassidy In 'Butch Cassidy And The Sundance Kid' (1969). His zodiac sign is Aquarius.
