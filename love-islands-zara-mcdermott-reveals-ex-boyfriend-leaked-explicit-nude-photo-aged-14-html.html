'It was a really horrible time in my life. I feel like between what happened back then and now seven years on there's been such a shift because now I'm seen as the victim whereas back then I was almost the perpetrator. I was the one in the wrong.'

Addressing the pressures on teenagers in this generation, she added: 'There were pictures of girls flying around everywhere.

'I think the reason why I got so badly bullied was because of the content of the image was incredibly explicit. There was a bit of pressure for me to do it. There was a hell a lot of pressure.' 

The fallout from the snap died out as she got older, but Zara suffered another blow when a second explicit video was leaked, during her time in the Love Island villa.

'It was someone who had never really treated me in that way before. I've been in relationships and this guy absolutely swept me off my feet.

Firm: When asked why she took more photos again after such lows at school, she said: 'He did ask me but it was a mutual thing. I don't look at them and I'm not ashamed or embarrassed'
