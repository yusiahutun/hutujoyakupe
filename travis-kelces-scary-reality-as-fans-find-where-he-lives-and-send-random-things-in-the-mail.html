Travis Kelce's scary reality as fans find where he lives and send random things in the mail

 Posted on April 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  452 words  |  Martina Birk

If any Travis Kelce fans reading this have sent him anything in the mail, he’s probably not received it, as he revealed he has to “turn all mail away” now.

Jason and Travis Kelce are back this week for a new episode of their New Heights podcast. Jason’s had a spring-clean this week, and his younger bro is so proud. However, he’s sharing the warnings of somebody ‘posting your house online’ for all to see.

Travis Kelce ‘sends all his fan mail back’

Jason Kelce revealed he and his wife, Kylie hired an organization company to help them declutter, which the Kansas City Chiefs star says “is the best.” He then shouts out ‘all the ladies’ that helped him in his KC house, but everything comes with a downside.

“The one thing you don’t realize when somebody posts your house online is that everybody now has your address,” the 34-year-old said on the latest New Heights episode. “People just send stuff to your house, so I literally stopped getting mail to my house.

“I had to stop, I had to literally tell the post office and everyone to stop bringing stuff to my house,” he explained.

However, Jason told his brother he’s missing out on some “pretty cool” stuff, but the Kansas City Chiefs player didn’t seem to care.

“Anything that comes to my house, send right back to the sender. Anybody that’s just sending random s*** to my house, it’s not getting to me,” he said.

When Trav first started dating Taylor Swift, he was reportedly ‘so overwhelmed’ with the attention that he had to move house to a new, safer one.

“He had to completely move out of his house,” Jason told The Big Podcast with Shaq. “People were just staying by his house. I mean, safety reasons.”

The NFL star may not be home for a while – as he’s set to tour Europe with Swift

After a well-deserved break, Swift will kick of the European leg of her Eras Tour this month, and Trav is reportedly going to hit the road with her.

The music star will be heading on a mammoth 52-date tour around the continent, and they’re said to be making it a family vacay, sources say, including their parents and Jason Kelce.

“The plan is for the families to link up at the beginning of May for the four-show run in France and will stay until May 30,” the source tells the US Sun.

“Travis is planning to stay on as the tour heads back to France and then the United Kingdom, while the families return to the United States,” the source claims.
