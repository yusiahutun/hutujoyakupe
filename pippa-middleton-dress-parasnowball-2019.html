Pippa Middleton Wore an Anna Mason Dress to the ParaSnowBall 2019

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  295 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • Pippa Middleton attended the ParaSnowBall on November 7 wearing a gorgeous gown by Anna Mason.
  • The chic dress is still available to shop for $1,405.
  • Middleton regularly attends the annual event hosted by Disability Snowsport.

We're often envious of Pippa Middleton's wardrobe, and her latest outfit is the perfect example of why.

The Duchess of Cambridge's sister made a chic appearance at the ParaSnowBall 2019, which is organized by Disability Snowsport and celebrates the achievements of Paralympic athletes. For the occasion, Middleton wore a beautiful Anna Mason gown, which somehow hasn't sold out just yet.

The tulle dress retails for $1,405, and features long sleeves and intricate floral embroidery. According to Twitter account @KateMCambridge, it's thought that Middleton's Emmy London clutch and shoes were borrowed from her sister, which is beyond adorable. She completed the look with an eye-catching gold belt and posed for photos with fellow attendees, looking amazing.

Of her involvement with the event, Middleton revealed, "I am honoured to be supporting Disability SnowSport UK and as a keen skier myself, I feel very passionately about enabling those with a disability to experience the joy of skiing alongside non-disabled people. The ParaSnowBall sponsored by Delancey was an inspirational celebration of Paralympians.”

Recently, Middleton opened up about her son, Arthur, who she's been taking for cranial osteopathy—a gentle massage on the skull meant to be calming—since he was just seven months old.
