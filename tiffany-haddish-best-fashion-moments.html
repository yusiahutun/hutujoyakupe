Tiffany Haddish Best Fashion Looks

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  644 words  |  Martina Birk

Tiffany Haddish caught the world's attention after starring in Girls Trip alongside Queen Latifah, Jada Pinkett-Smith, and Regina Hall this past summer. She was funny. She was fab. And she made us think: We need to know more about this woman!

Now, the 37-year-old actress is back in the spotlight thanks to one outstanding and very funny performance as the host of Saturday Night Live this past weekend. Haddish made television history by becoming the first-ever black female stand-up comic to host the show. Mic drop.

She delivered one LOL-worthy joke after the next, tackled sexual harassment and Donald Trump, and hilariously described her $4,000 Alexander McQueen dress, which she refuses to stop wearing. Over the summer Haddish wore the white dress for a promotional Girls Trip event and her team advised her that it's taboo to publicly repeat a dress.

"I don't give a dang about no taboo. I spent a lot of money on this dress. This dress cost way more than my mortgage. This a Alexander McQueen, OK?" she said during her monologue and explained that she would re-wear it "multiple times." Later on in the show, she wore during the Weekend Update sketch.

Yes, she's funny, but our point is that Haddish is fast becoming our new style crush. On the show, she delivered head-turning looks that put forth her confidence and risk-taking style.
