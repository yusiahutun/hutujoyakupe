Nearly-nude model wanders around a baseball stadium in a thong and body paint

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  770 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A model received plenty of stares and even a stop from security after she showed up to a baseball stadium wearing nothing but a thong and paint on just the front half of her body.

Body painter Jen Seidel has a talent for making women look like they are wearing clothes when they're not, but for her latest prank, she decided to try something different and only cover her participant's front side.

While model Ashley Nicole was painted to look like she was wearing a skimpy tank top and frayed denim shorts, she was left naked from behind aside from her nude thong.

Nearly nude: Body painters Jen Seidel (right) and Kennedy Walsh (left) only painted half of model Ashley Nicole's body for their latest prank 

Lifelike: Ashley's front was painted to look like she was wearing a skimpy tank top and frayed denim shorts

'Today we body painted Ashley and we're going in public, but the kick is she's not painted in the back. She's completely bare,' body painter Kennedy Walsh says at the start of the clip.

'That's the first time that we have done this,' Jen adds. 

Ashley is sent to walk outside Oriole Park at Camden Yards in Baltimore, Maryland, and one woman speaks too soon when she tells her, 'I like your outfit.' 

As soon as she walks by and the woman catches sight of her backside, her jaw drops in shock.

'Did you see that too?' someone else asks while pointing at the half-painted model walking in front of him. 

Despite the stares and double takes she is getting, Ashley does a great job of looking nonchalant. She even goes out of her way to say hi as she makes her way through the throng of people.

Many of the double takes come from men who are either completely befuddled or can't stop staring.

'She's naked,' one guy is overheard saying.

Another man is so entranced by Ashley's derrière that he drops something he is holding and has to stoop down to pick it back up.

At one point, Ashley is stopped by security and is asked if she is going to the baseball game. She tells the man she is just walking to a tail gate and greeting people along the way, which he says is perfectly fine. 

'Security came and we thought we were f****d but turns out they just wanted to see a naked girl,' Kennedy tells the camera. 

Ashley is eventually sent on her merry way, but one security guard gives her backside a long look as he drives by.

As the security guards sit by and watch, one woman shouts that Ashley is 'beautiful' while another onlooker adds that her outfit is 'so cool.' There are even people who ask to take pictures with her.

At the end of the video, three guys offer to pay money to take a snapshot with Ashley, and while she obliges, she warns them to keep their 'hands off the paint.'

They each take a photo with her, and after two of them run off, the remaining stranger reaches into his wallet and gives Ashley a $5 bill.

'That was awkward,' she tells the camera. 'I was like please don't grab my a**.'
