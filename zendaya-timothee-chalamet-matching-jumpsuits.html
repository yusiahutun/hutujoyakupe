Zendaya and Timothe Chalamet Are Pastel Twins in His-and-Hers Jumpsuits

 Posted on April 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  332 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Dune: Part Two press tour has officially touched down in South Korea, and the movie’s lead stars brought their unmatched red carpet style with them.

Timothée Chalamet and Zendaya showed up for a press conference Wednesday in Seoul wearing matching leather jumpsuits from Korean designer Juun.J’s Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear collection. While the model who originally wore the look on the runway styled it with the front zipped down and sleeves wrapped around the waist to reveal a structured turtleneck bodysuit underneath, Zendaya and Chalamet eschewed the turtleneck, and put their own stamps on the unique pieces.

Zendaya, who stepped out in a peachy pastel-pink iteration, wore hers slightly unzipped down her chest and with one sleeve drooping down her shoulder. She tied off the belt to cinch her waist, and finished off the look with matching pink pointed-toe boots and a dainty pendant necklace.

Han Myung-Gu//Getty Images

Chalamet looked cool in a baby-blue version of the jumpsuit. He unzipped the front to about halfway down his chest, revealing a few layers of thin silver chain necklaces, and capped off the ensemble with chunky sky-blue boots.

ANTHONY WALLACE//Getty Images

The Dune duo last made a splash at the world premiere of the film in London last week. Zendaya wowed in a chrome armor suit with clear panels across the torso, arms, legs, and rear—a showstopping vintage piece from Mugler’s Fall/Winter 1995 couture collection. For an extra splash of extravagance, she topped off the look with a diamond Bulgari necklace.

Chalamet appeared in a comparatively casual outfit, opting for an oversize black T-shirt and crinkled metallic silver pants by Haider Ackermann.
