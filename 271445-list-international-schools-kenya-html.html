List of international schools in Kenya

 Posted on February 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  530 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kenya is home to a good number of international schools were your kids can thrive and get top-notch education. Would you like a list of international schools in Kenya? if so, this article is for you. We have highlighted the best institutions where you can be guaranteed of quality education.

Kenya has a broad range of international schools to provide an outstanding education to students. There are international schools in Nairobi that follow the national curriculum of Sweden, UK, Japan, USA, the Netherlands, France and Germany.

List of international schools in Kenya

Here is a list of best international schools in Kenya:

Brookhouse School

Brookhouse School or Brookhouse International School is a co-educational day and boarding school offering Early Years, preparatory and secondary schooling which follows the British National Curriculum. Students take examinations through both the Edexcel and Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) Boards. It was founded in 1981 and is located in Langata, a suburb of Nairobi, about 10 km (6 min) from Nairobi's CBD.

Nairobi International School

The vision of Nairobi International School is to provide technology-based education to students of all religions, races and nationalities through choices and responsibilities, fellowship and by giving opportunities to lead a life of learning through dedicated study. At the same time, committing themselves to personal growth, self-discipline, integrity, and caring for each other, as “Creative thinkers”.

Rusinga school

Rusinga School is a Christian day, which follows the British National Curriculum. It is located at Gitanga Road. The address is P.O. BOX 25088, Nairobi 00603.

Braeburn School

Braeburn School is a co-educational international school, which follows the British National Curriculum. It was established in 1979. The vision of this school is that every student becomes a confident individual, a successful learner, and a responsible citizen. This school is divided into five sections

  • Early Years Foundation Stage
  • Primary - Key Stages 1 and 2
  • Lower Secondary - Key Stage 3
  • Secondary - Key Stage 4 (IGCSE)
  • Sixth Form - Key Stage 5 (A-Level, IB, BTEC).

Their schools are located in Nairobi, Kisumu, Mombasa, Nanyuki, Dar-es-salaam and Arusha.

Peponi School

Peponi School is an international school, which was founded in 1989 on a 45-acre piece of land. This school provides an outstanding education for its students. Peponi School is located in Ruiru, Kenya.

West Nairobi School

West Nairobi School is one of the American international schools in Kenya, which follows American curriculum. It was founded in 1996. It is a co-ed day school from Kindergarten to high school. For students OF 4 – 5 years old, WNS has Early Childhood Learning Center. The vision of this school is to create life-long learners by providing an education of excellence. West Nairobi School is located in Karen, Nairobi City, Kenya.

Kenya High School

Kenya high school is a public girl’s high school, which is located in Kileleshwa suburb in Nairobi, Kenya. About 1300 students were enrolled in Kenya high school each year. The school was renamed from the Kenya European Girls' High School to the Kenya High School. The school was established in 1908.
