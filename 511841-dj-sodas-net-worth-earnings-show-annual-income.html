Career as an artist

DJ Soda began her career as a musician after signing a recording deal with Warner Music Group. Shortly after joining the label, she released her debut EP, Closer, in June 2015.

After the release, she teamed up with the Sistar, a South Korean girl group and performed at several events, including S20 Songkran Music Festival in Bangkok, Thailand. In 2018, she left Warner Music Group and joined House of Music.

Shortly after, the sublabel became defunct; thus, she was transferred to Highline Entertainment, another Starship sublabel. In June 2018, she participated in the Ultra Music Festival Korea (UMF). The following month, she released Never Let You In, featuring Walshy Fire.
