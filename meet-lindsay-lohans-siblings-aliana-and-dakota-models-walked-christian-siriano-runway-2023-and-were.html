If there’s one thing we know about the Lohans, it’s how tight of a bond they share. At Lindsay’s Irish Wish film premiere in New York last month, her family came out in force to support her, including her husband Bader Shammas.

Lindsay took to Instagram to share the love, posting a set of pictures with her crew and the heartfelt caption, “So grateful for all of the love and support.”

So what do we know about Lindsay Lohan’s younger siblings, Aliana and Dakota?

Aliana Lohan

Growing up in the Lohan family means Aliana is no stranger to the limelight. Per People, like her siblings Lindsay and Michael Jnr, she signed with Ford Models when she was still young.

While she may have been in the background of some of Lindsay’s cinematic ventures – from 1998’s The Parent Trap to the thriller I Know Who Killed Me in 2007 – Aliana’s artistic aspirations weren’t confined to brief appearances.

At only nine, Aliana released her first album, Lohan Holiday. Since then, she’s put out singles including “All the Way Around”, “Without You” and “You’re Not Gonna Tempt Me” – the latter being the reality TV show Temptation Island’s theme song for four seasons.

Aliana is also a model. In 2023, she walked the runway with her brother Dakota at the Christian Siriano fashion show. Speaking to People in 2023, she said her creative pursuits were intrinsic to her identity, regardless of her sister’s towering fame. “I would’ve been doing this with or without my sister being Lindsay Lohan,” she said.

Alongside the rest of her family, she’s also been featured in reality TV shows including E! True Hollywood Story, Living Lohan and Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club.

Yet it was in Netflix’s Falling for Christmas, which stars sister Lindsay, that her musical contributions started to shine. Aliana has a small role in the film, and her song “Without You” is also featured in the soundtrack. “I wrote that song about a year ago and then it was placed beautifully in the film,” she told Drew Barrymore on her talk show. “I’m very grateful,” she added.

Dakota Lohan

Like Aliana, Dakota Lohan is a model who walked the runway for Christian Siriano in 2023. Dakota – often referred to as “Cody” – signed with IMG Models in 2016, but is now with One Management, according to People. In 2017, he appeared in a Vogue China spread, prompting Lindsay to tweet how proud she was of him.

In an interview with W Magazine in 2016, he talked about taking the plunge into modelling, crediting his family’s support. “I’m a student and a surfer – I’ve always put both of those interests first – but it was my family who gave me the confidence to undertake modelling.”

Dakota has a role in the film, and wrote on Instagram that he was “beyond grateful to have had the opportunity to work with the amazing cast and crew that were part of this film, especially my sister.”
