Who is Skylar Neese?

Skylar Neese was born on February 10, 1996, and was the only child of David and Mary Neese. Skylar's mother worked as an administrative assistant in a cardiac laboratory, and her father, David, worked as a product assembler at Walmart.

Neese attended University High School and was a great student. According to her parents, she was a high achiever in academia, excelling in almost every subject, regardless of whether she liked them or not.

Skylar’s disappearance

Skylar’s story is one of the most puzzling, considering the young personalities involved in her murder case. How could such young people perform the unimaginable act? Well, you will be surprised at how the story starts and builds up to the eventual murder.

Skylar Neese had two best friends, Shelia Eddy and Rachel Shoaf. However, it wasn't always the three of them. Skylar met Eddy when they were in elementary school, and Shoaf joined them during their freshman year in high school. And that is where things start getting interesting.

As you might have guessed, the once inseparable duo of Skylar and Shelia was no more, as Rachel made it a trio. The new friendship drove a wedge between Skylar and Eddy, but they coped as friends do. After all, it was about supporting one another.

However, it was never a balanced friendship after Shoaf’s arrival as Neese started competing with her to be the closest friend of Eddy, who liked the attention. Also, Neese served as an emotional rock for the trio as both Eddy and Shoaf had divorced parents.

On July 5, 2012, Skylar Neese snuck out of her parent’s home in Morgantown, West Virginia, to hang out with her friends. They resided on the ground floor, and Skylar used a bench under her bedroom window to climb out, meeting her two friends, Rachel and Eddy, outside waiting in a car.

Skylar's parents found out that their daughter had sneaked out through the window the following morning. And at the window was the bench she used to reach the exit. Also, surveillance footage showed Neese climbing out of the window.

And that was the beginning of the nightmare, which necessitated the involvement of the police. Skylar's parents called the precinct to report her disappearance. Her friends claimed that they had dropped her home at around midnight.

What happened to the killers of Skylar Neese?
