Enjoying some time off: The reality star, 47, took a vacation after leaving The Real Housewives Of Atlanta

Lately, NeNe's career has been in the midst of major transition.

In addition to confirming she would not be returning to The Real Housewives of Atlanta, her upcoming reality show with co-star Kim Zolciak, NeNe and Kim: Road to Riches, was scrapped by Bravo before a single episode aired because of problems with scheduling, according to TMZ.

The star was busy with a new project, which she announced prior to the show's cancellation.

Moving on: NeNe, pictured on July 24, took to her blog in earlier last month to reveal her new role opposite Betty White

NeNe took to her blog in early July to reveal her new role opposite Betty White: 'When one door closes, another door opens!!!! Your girl is returning to primetime network TV on the new ABC variety series To Tell the Truth with Betty White and Anthony Anderson.'

In To Tell The Truth - a reboot of a 60s game show - three contestants must try to convince NeNe, Betty and a third panelist that they invented something or discovered something.

Two of the contestants are lying and one is telling the truth and the panelists must ask questions to figure it out.
