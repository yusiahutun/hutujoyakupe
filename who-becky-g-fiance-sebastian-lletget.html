MUCH loved musician Becky G is engaged to FC Dallas player Sebastian Lletget.

The Shower singer has been dating the soccer star since 2016 and the couple made the happy announcement in December 2022.

Who is Becky G's fiancé?

Born on September 3, 1992, Sebastian Lletget is an American-Argentinian soccer player from San Francisco.

In September 2010, Lletget signed his first professional contract with West Ham United and made his debut on January 5, 2014.

On May 8, 2015, he was signed by LA Galaxy and stayed with the team for six years.

Lletget also played a short stint with the New England Revolution.

When did Sebastian Lletget propose to Becky G?

On December 9, 2022, Lletget and his longtime girlfriend Becky G revealed they were engaged after six years of dating.

In a series of photos posted to Instagram, they shared pictures of the moment when the midfielder proposed and the singer captioned the shots: "Our spot forever," with a white heart emoji.

Regarding her relationship with Lletget, Becky G told E! News: "Although we are very open about our relationship, we're also still very private at the same time.

"Fans haven't been able to see that side to our relationship.

"They see Seb as the professional athlete that he is and they see me as the professional artist that I am but at the end of the day, we are each other's best friends."

How did Becky G meet Sebastian Lletget?

While she was filming the Power Rangers movie in early 2016, Becky G met Lletget through mutual friends Naomi Scott and Jordan Spence.

Becky and Naomi were co-stars in the superhero flick.

Meanwhile, Lletget and Spence played for West Ham United together.

The Becky From The Block singer explained to Mega 96.3 via J-14: "[Our introduction] kind of came about while we were filming the movie.

"The way we got introduced, me and Sebastian, was through my costar Naomi.

"Her husband — she's 23, they've been married for a couple of years now — and [he] played soccer together, him and Sebastian.

"So Nae met me and was like, 'Girl, you're awesome! You deserve to be with a man!'"

She also mentioned she was hesitant about her friend's attempts at matchmaking and shared: "I was like, 'Nah, I don't want to be with anybody. I've been there, done that [with] the relationship thing. I'm focused right now. I'm good. I'm good.'

"She's like, 'It's fine, it's fine, I already know who you're going to marry. It's fine, it's fine.'

"She's talking to her husband and he's like, 'Oh yeah, Sebby, Sebby,' and I'm like, 'who's this Sebby guy? What are they talking about?'

"Then she shows me a video of him and I'm trying to downplay it like because I'm like, 'He's fine, good-looking.'

"It kind of all led to that. He came out of nowhere. I followed him on Twitter and he followed me back, and I remember it was like this ghosty, 'I know you exist, you know I exist' thing."

The musician added: "I waited a couple of days after my 19th birthday just to sound older, which is so funny to me now.

"He was like 23 at the time. So I slid in the DM real hard and I was like, 'Hi, what's up?' So groundbreaking!

"Honestly, we became friends first. We were vibing so hard.
