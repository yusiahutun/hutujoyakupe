Who is Arnetta Yardbourgh?

The American businesswoman has not shared details about her parents. She prefers to keep her personal life away from the limelight. Similarly, there is no information about her siblings and early childhood.

After completing her primary education in Houston, Texas, she joined Judson High School in San Antonio from 1987 to 1990. Here is the list of her other educational qualifications:
