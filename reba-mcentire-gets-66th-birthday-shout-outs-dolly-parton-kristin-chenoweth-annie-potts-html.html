 Posted on March 23, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  657 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Three-time Grammy winner Reba McEntire quietly celebrated her 66th birthday on Sunday with a few shout-outs from her famous friends.

10-time Grammy winner Dolly Parton shared an autographed picture of herself with the flame-haired country crooner, whom she called 'a true musician' and 'a true friend.'

Tony winner Kristin Chenoweth, who sang I Am Woman with Reba and Jennifer Hudson on her 2019 record For the Girls, called Sunday 'International Reba Day.'
