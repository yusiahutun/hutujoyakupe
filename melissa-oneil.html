Who Is Melissa O’Neil? Bio

Melissa O’Neil was born on July 12, 1988, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, as Melissa Crystal O’Neil. Moreover, she is the daughter of Alison Yeung (Mother) and Tim O’Neil (Father). O’Neil belongs to white ethnicity and holds Canadian nationality.

In addition, her star sign is Cancer. Melissa attended Terry Fox Junior High School and later shifted to Lester B. Pearson High School.

Melissa O’Neil’s Career & Achievements

Melissa O’Neil had a passion for entertainment and decided to give audition at the third season of Canadian Idol. Who knew that her life was going to change from there. After her audition, the judges, Farley Flex, Sass Jordan, Jake Gold, and Zack Werner gave her standing ovations.

Not to mention, O’Neil easily made it through to the top 100 and then top 32 and ultimately the winner of the competition. That is not only her achievement but she also made a record of the youngest and first female to win the show.

Moreover, O’Neil released her first album titled “Melissa O’Neil” in 2005. It reached number sixteen on the Canadian albums chart. O’Neil is not only a singer but she is also an actress. Likewise, she made her film debut in 2010 by appearing in Broken Hearts and further starred as a guest in Camelot.
