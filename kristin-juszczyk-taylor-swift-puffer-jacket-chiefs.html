Kristin Juszczyk Shares the Details Behind Taylor Swift's Puffer Jacket

 Posted on May 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Anyone who watched the Wild Card Game between the Kansas City Chiefs and the Miami Dolphins noticed one prominent storyline: The weather. At kickoff, the temperature was negative 4 degrees, with a windchill of negative 27, making it the fourth coldest game in NFL history. Fans who braved the game (even the most famous attendees who had the luxury of watching from a box) had to seriously bundle up.

Taylor Swift, who cheered on her boyfriend Travis Kelce and the Chiefs to a 26-7 win, made a stylish appearance at the game, as we've come to expect. But this time she stayed warm thanks to a Chiefs puffer jacket made by talented designer Kristin Juszczyk. Football fans will recognize Kristin's name: She is married to San Francisco 49ers fullback, Kyle Juszczyk.

Jamie Squire / Kristin Juszczyk

Kristin often makes clothes to wear from the games, repurposing garments into incredible designs, and she's dressed Simone Biles and 49ers star Deebo Samuel. She made herself a jersey puffer coat last week, but she calls creating jackets for Taylor and Patrick Mahomes's wife Brittany "an honor of a lifetime."

Kristin shared the process of making the coats "strictly out of jerseys." She added embroidered details and an extra-thick liner to keep Taylor and Brittany warm.
