Reindeer Boobs craze makes VERY sexy return for Christmas 2018

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  410 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Fashion fans aren’t planning on dressing up warmly this year.

While some are flaunting festive vajazzles, others have turned their bare bums into baubles.

This isn’t the only saucy style to make a comeback in winter 2018.

The popular Reindeer Boob craze is likely to re-emerge ahead of December 25.

Daily Star Online caught up with the creator of the kinky trend to find out more.

Reindeer Boobs surfaced in 2013 when Your Sassy Grandma started selling cheeky Christmas jumpers online.

The Etsy store began flogging appliqués that allow you to transform your breasts into Rudolph.

Customers are urged to stick a red pom pom over their nipples, with googly eyes and antlers completing the look.

Body confident shoppers can flaunt the look by going topless.

Alternatively, they can purchase a festive jumper that provides warmth while flashing a portion of chest.

Your Sassy Grandma business owner, Tyler Sheri Wiseman, recently spoke to Daily Star Online about her inspiration.

She revealed: “Back in 2012 we had an ugly Christmas sweater party, it was kind of the beginning of the ugly sweater party trend.

“As you can imagine we had a hard time finding ugly sweaters to wear, so being the crafty people we are we just made some.

“Then next year we had another sweater party. This time I got online and really searched for unique ideas.

“I saw a picture of a person with a similar reindeer boob so I hunted online to see if anyone was selling such a thing.

“No one had anything even remotely similar, no reindeer, no pasties, no reindeer boob sweaters, etc.

“So we drew up a bunch of prototypes, researched how to make pasties etc. This is the design we came up with.”

When Tyler began selling the unique jumpers, she was immediately met with a positive response.

She explained: “The image went viral with over a million views – hence the start of the 'Reindeer Boob'.

“Last year, hundreds of people began to make their own copies of the Reindeer Boob. It went viral on Instagram.

“We never could have imagined that we would have started such a trend!”

If you’re brave enough to rock the look, you can purchase the products from Your Sassy Grandma’s Etsy page.

The store is selling burlesque-style nipple pasties for £20.

You can also purchase boob-exposing jumpers from £40.

Customers seem very happy with their products, with the page racking up 134 five star reviews.
