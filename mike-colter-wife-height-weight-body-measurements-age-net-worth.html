Mike Colter Brief Bio, Age

Mike Colter graduated from Calhoun County High School, where he was named the “most ambitious” student in his senior year. After spending a year at Benedict College, Colter moved on and received his BA in Theater Studies from the University of South Carolina, to which he was transferred in 1999. He continued his education at the Mason Gross School of the Arts at Rutgers University, where he earned an MFA degree in acting.
