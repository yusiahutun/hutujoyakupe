Widely recognized for winning the Playmate of the Year, Karen McDougal is a preschool teacher turned model. After she won a swimwear competition her career in modeling flourished landing her opportunities to feature in many magazines, commercials, and other media. She also became the first woman to appear on the cover of Men’s Fitness. She is currently working as a fitness model and a columnist for OK Magazine, Radar Online, and Star Magazine. Early Life And Education of Karen Mcdougal

Karen McDougal was born in Merrillville, Indiana, near Gary, on March 23, 1971. She belongs to American nationality and is of Mixed ethnicity (Cherokee Indian and Irish descent). When McDougal was 9, her mother, Carol McDougal, remarried after which the family moved to Sawyer, Michigan. She has 3 older brothers, Bob McDougal, Dave McDougal, and Jeff McDougal, and a younger sister, Tina McDougal. Growing up in Merrillville, Indiana, she studied ballet and tap dance with a dream to become a ballerina.

McDougal was a cheerleader, band member, and a member of the color guard, during her high school. At the time, she was also heavily involved in sports, played volleyball and softball. Her high school nickname was “Barbie” because she was considered wholesome and sweet. She graduated from high school in 1989. 

McDougal later attended Ferris State University at Big Rapids, Michigan, majored in Elementary Education. After studying for a couple of years in college, she began working as a preschool teacher in Detroit, Michigan.

Karen Mcdougal's Professional Career

Modeling

McDougal went on to compete in the local Venus Swimwear swimsuit competition in Michigan which she won, earning a spot at the international finals in Florida. After the competition, she caught the eye of Playboy photographer David Mecey who approached her for a test shoot at Playboy Studio West.  

Karen McDougal got a chance to try out for Playboy, in the late 1990s. The American businessman and magazine publisher Hugh Hefner announced McDougal as the 1998 Playmate of the Year. For winning the Playmate of the Year, she also received $100,000 and a special edition silver Shelby series 1 convertible with a customized Michigan license plate "PMOY 98".

McDougal then featured on the cover of many following issues of Playboy. She also featured on The Magic Hour to promote her PMOY issue. In a poll conducted by Ranker, she listed number one on the hottest Playmate of the 90s.

McDougal was the first woman to feature on the cover of Men’s Fitness magazine, in March 1999. She expanded her career in modeling by featuring in fitness and several bodybuilding magazines, including Muscle & Fitness in January 2000, Physical in June 2004 and Iron Man.

In 2002, Karen McDougal released a limited edition doll based on her likeness. After a couple of years, she appeared in a photo spread in a fashion and lifestyle magazine Vogue, alongside fellow Playmates, Pamela Anderson, Tishara Cousin, and Audra Lynn.

Following her success as Playmate of the Year, McDougal featured in various pin-up posters, magazine covers, calendars, advertising campaigns, clothing, swimwear, promotional events, and lingerie catalogs.

Film And TV

In addition to her contribution to modeling, Karen McDougal has also appeared in several TV programs and played a number of significant roles in movies. Her acting credits include Joe Dirt, Charlie's Angels, Grind, The Girl Next Door, Lovespring International, Anger Management, The O'Reilly Factor.

According to her Instagram bio, McDougal works as a fitness model and a columnist for OK Magazine, Radar Online, and Star Magazine.

Other Work And Hobbies

In 2010, McDougal became one of the prime shareholders of Pharmore Alternatives, a company selling various health and wellness supplements. At the beginning of 2011, she attended the 2011 AVN Adult Entertainment Expo as a promoter.

Karen McDougal loves riding motorcycle and is an avid car collector. She bought a pink custom-built motorcycle, in 2004. The motorcycle received the Best Radical Custom award. She is also an animal supporter who has a small puppy named Shihtzu.
