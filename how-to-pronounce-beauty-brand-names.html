How to pronounce beauty brand names

 Posted on February 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Larita Shotwell

From the brand names that are intended to start conversations (way to go, Ouai), to the ones that require A level French execution (Biologique Recherche, we're looking at you), many popular products can catch out even the most beauty astute amongst us. To ensure you're talking the talk correctly, swot up on Bazaar's complete guide to beauty brand and fashion house fragrance pronunciation. Practice makes perfect...
