Brie Larson and Carla Bruni look sensational as they lead stars at Chopard Trophy dinner in Cannes

 Posted on January 28, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1547 words  |  Martina Birk

Brie Larson and Carla Bruni were the epitome of class as they both stunned in black ensembles at the Chopard Trophy event in Cannes on Friday.

Brie, 33, was a vision in a black semi-transparent gown as she attended the award at the 76th Annual Cannes Film Festival at Carlton Beach.

The floor-length dress perfectly hugged her slender figure and flaunted her toned arms.

The Captain Marvel star left a little to the imagination as she went braless - as the stunning gown featured sparkling black cats details on the skirt fabric.

Brie oozed class as she accessorised the look with a set of diamond jewellery, including drop pendant earrings, a matching bracelet and two rings.

She showcased her taut visage as she opted for a natural and subtle make up look, adding a final touch with a bright red lipstick that matched her nails.

Meanwhile Carla Bruni looked commanded attention in a black strapless feather-trimmed dress, that flaunted her toned physique.

The French fashion model, 55, brought her impeccable sense of style to the event - oozing confidence as she posed up with her typical pose holding her face.

The acclaimed cover-girl looked stunning as she showcased a dazzling set of diamond jewellery to match the black ensemble.

Carla donned an eye-catching diamond choker necklace, flaunting her slender neck - and teamed it with matching flashy rings and a bracelet.

The fashion icon gave a final touch of glamour as she wore black stiletto heels, featuring a heart-shape made of red and fuchsia rhinestones.

The beauty looked glowing as she styled her chocolate tresses into an up-do bun, leaving her fringe down and some locks to frame her stunning features.
