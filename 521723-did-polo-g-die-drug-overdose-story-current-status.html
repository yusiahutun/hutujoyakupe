Who is Polo G?

Polo G is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and record executive who rose to fame with the singles Finer Things (2018) and Pop Out (2019). The star has amassed a considerable fanbase due to his style of rap involving drill, trap and conscious hip hop.

Taurus Tremani Bartlett, also known as Polo G, was born on 6 January 1999 in Old Town, Chicago, Illinois, USA. He is 24 years old (as of 2023), and his zodiac sign is Capricorn. The rapper's parents are Taurus Bartlett and Stacia Mac. Polo is the second born in his family and has three siblings: two sisters and one brother.

Taurus attended George Manierre Elementary School in Chicago, Illinois. He then proceeded to a military school, where he transferred to Wendell Philips Academy High School in Bronzeville. He didn't last in Wendell, moving to his third school, Innovations High School, an art school in Chicago.

Did Polo G die?

No, he didn't. However, the rapper had a near-fatal health scare on 12 August 2019. According to XXL, Polo G posted photos of himself in the hospital after a night out, which resulted in his hospitalisation. In the now-deleted post, the rapper revealed that he nearly lost his life.

Why did Polo G go to the hospital? According to Rap-Up, the rapper had overdosed on some Onnem pills. He admitted that he had no recollection of what had happened the previous night, and his father had to fill him in.

How many pills did Polo G take? The star revealed that he had taken 17 tablets in one day. After the incident, the rapper quit taking the recreational medications, especially since he had just welcomed his son earlier in 2019.

Was Polo G found dead?

No, he wasn't. Responders rushed him to hospital after overdosing on some pills during a party. The rumour that the rapper had died was an internet hoax.
