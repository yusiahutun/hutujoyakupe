Dismembered human penis found in the parking lot of an Alabama gas station

 Posted on February 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  492 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A human penis has been found in the parking lot of a gas station in Alabama and may be tied to a deadly crash in which a motorcyclist was 'struck by multiple vehicles.'

Customers wandering into the gas station on Interstate 10 in Mobile were shocked to find the rogue appendage according to local newspaper Lagniappe.

Mobile Police Department told the outlet that the bizarre discovery was not being investigated as a murder or assault but could be connected to the devastating events which saw 29-year-old Christopher Means lose his life.

Means was pronounced dead at the scene after losing control of his motorbike and being struck by several vehicles with only 'one driver' remaining at the scene.

Police officers responded to a motorcycle crash around 6.00 am on Monday near eastbound I-10 between I-65 and Dauphin Island Parkway, according to the MPD.

Officers found one of the drivers involved, Means, dead at the scene of the crash.

'The preliminary investigation revealed that the victim was riding his motorcycle Southbound on the I-65 ramp to Eastbound I-10 when he lost control of the motorcycle,' according to a police statement.

'The rider was thrown from his motorcycle and struck by multiple vehicles.'

The I-10 eastbound lanes had shut down for hours, after four separate car crashes that morning and MPD have said they're looking for the drivers who unknowingly ran over Means' body and kept going.
