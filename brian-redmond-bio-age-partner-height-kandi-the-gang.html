Meet Brian Redmond, one of the stars of BravoTV’s Atlanta-based new reality show Kandi & The Gang. The show follows the drama at RHOA alum Kandi Burruss’s restaurant Old Lady Gang (OLG). Learn more about his below.

Here we cover Brian’s age, partner, height, and family.

Brian Redmond On Kandi & The Gang

“Kandi & The Gang” is a new BravoTV reality show with a similar idea to “Vanderpump Rules.” However, it differs from the latter show in one area: gay guys. The show follows the controversy at Atlanta-based eatery Old Lady Gang (OLG), whose owner is Kandi Burruss of the Real Housewives of Atlanta.

The new show has gotten a lot of buzz because of its unusual cast, which includes members of the LGBTQ+ community who also happen to be Kandi’s employees.

“When I realized that we did something different than anybody else was when the commercial [for ‘Kandi & the Gang] came out,” speaking about the show Kandi told PageSix.

“I saw people in the comments saying, ‘Wow, this is dope that they are showing this and I feel included now.’ I was like, ‘Wow,’” Kandi added. “We all exist, right? But we’re not necessarily all represented when it comes to television. So come on to ‘Kandi & The Gang.’ We’re going to make everybody feel right at home.”

One of the cast members of the show is Brian Redmond. He’s the former head bartender at OLG who’s now working as a server. Brian, the consummate entertainer, makes everyone laugh at their tables as well as on stage, where he does stand-up comedy. Brian started his own business outside of the restaurant, and he’s excited to incorporate his specialty food dishes onto the OLG menu.

Besides the three, other casts joining the show are Kandi’s mom, Mama Joyce, and two aunts Nora and Bertha. We also get to meet DonJuan Clark, Kandi’s right hand, restaurant developer Phillip Frempong, self-proclaimed original hostess Shawndreca Robinson, “day one” bartender Dom’Unique Variety, OLG manager Brandon Black, parking lot manager, and Kandi’s cousin Patrick Dallas, Torin Mitchell the event manager, kitchen manager Melvin jones, and host Rashard Roles.

On 21 January 2022, he announced on his Instagram, “Ooommggggg!!!! It’s here!! Buckle ya seatbelts beloveds!!!! #kandiandthegang @kandi @todd167 @donjuannc @bravotv @bravoandy @lhlawson18 thank uuuuuuuuuuu”.

Brian Redmond Net Worth

Brian Redmond has a net worth under $500 thousand.

According to Open Corporates, he served as the agent for The Original Soul Roll LLC (@theoriginalsoulroll), a company that was registered on 19 September 2021. Besides that, he has also been providing credit repair service with “a 90% success rate and works around the clock to get your negative trade lines removed in 30-45 days”.

Back in California, Brian also worked at the Automobile Club of Southern California as an insurance agent.

Has Brian Redmond Revealed His Family?

Brian Redmond was born to his father Grand Parker Redmond II (@grant.redmond.545).

In July 2017, Brian sadly announced that his father died. He posted on his IG, “Just found out my Dad passed…Finally got ur wings Pops! Love u to the stars and back…..So glad we were so tight and I got to have you in my life always accepting me for who I am and didn’t want me to change a bit. Now Rest in Heaven! Love uuuuuuuuu Grant Parker Redmond.”

According to his father’s obituary, Grant was born on 14 March 1951 and died on 25 July 2017. As for his mother, he has only featured her once on her birthday on 30 August.

In his family, Brian has two sisters — A’ja and Charlotte (@charlotte.n.redmond). He also has four brothers — Noah, Donald, Uriah, and Grant Parker III (@grantredmond3). Grant III lives in LA, California, and works at JPMorgan Chase & Co.

Grant has six nephews and nieces; Donald, Chloe, Zahra, Sumaiyah, Zendaya, and Kingston. He has an uncle named Ronald (@ronald.redmond.39).
