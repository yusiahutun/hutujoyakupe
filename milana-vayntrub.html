Introduction

Milana Vayntrub is an Uzbekistan-born American actress who is best known as Lily from AT&T commercials.

Biography

She was born on March 8, 1987, in Tashkent, Uzbek SSR, USSR [now Uzbekistan], as Milana Aleksandrovna Vayntrub.

At age 2, she immigrated to West Hollywood with her parents as refugees.

”Both my parents were born there, but we were considered outsiders,” Milana told NBC News in 2016. ”We had some family in LA who offered to help us get there. So when they got the chance to leave, my parents took it.”

Milana said her family stopped in Austria and Italy before they arrived in the United States.

”Once here, my parents worked their butts off to give me a good education and upbringing,” she said in an interview. ”They did a great job.”

At age 5, Vayntrub started acting in Mattel Barbie commercials.

@Getty

In 1995, she made her acting debut appearing in three episodes of the NBC medical drama television series ”ER,” featuring George Clooney as Dr. Doug Ross. 

In 1996, Milana appeared in ”Days of Our Lives.”

Vayntrub earned a Bachelor of Arts in Communication from the University of California, San Diego.

In September 2011, Milana and actress Stevie Nelson created the YouTube channel – ”LivePrudeGirls.”

AT&T

© Getty Images

In November 2013, AT&T picked Milana to appear in their commercials, where she plays Lily Adams, a fictional AT&T salesperson.

In 2015, she performed as Tina Shukshin in eight episodes of the TV series – ”Other Space.”

In 2016, Vayntrub directed her first ad commercial for ”Cracker Barrel Macaroni & Cheese.”

”Commercial directing felt like a very natural transition from my comedy, sketch, music video directing experience,” Milana said.

Can’t Do Nothing

© Getty Images

In 2016, she established an organization called ”Can’t Do Nothing” after a vacation in Greece opened her eyes to Syrian refugees risking their lives to cross the Mediterranean Sea.

Her organization is ”designed to help everyday individuals have a real positive impact and assist refugees worldwide.”

”I remember being an outsider, and the transition that my parents had to go through, learning the language and trying to get work,” Vayntrub told HuffPost in February 2017. She added: ”So I definitely feel close to the plight of an immigrant, let alone the plight of a refugee.”

From 2016 to 2017, Milana portrayed Sloane Sandburg in ”This Is Us,” a heartwarming and emotional story in which also stars Niles Fitch, Milo Ventimiglia, Alexandra Breckenridge, Mandy Moore, Jon Huertas, Sterling K. Brown, Chris Sullivan, and Susan Kelechi Watson.

In 2017, she performed as Jill in the Eko interactive web television series That Moment When.

Starting in 2018, Milana has voiced Squirrel Girl in the Marvel Rising franchise.
