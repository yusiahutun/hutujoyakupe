British actress Kelly Reilly is best known for her as Mary Watson - Dr. Watson's (portrayed by Jude Law) wife - in the Sherlock Holmes (2009/ 2011) film series. Further, she is also famous for her role as the notorious Caroline Bingley in the 2005's hit Pride and Prejudice.

Most recently, the talented actress is enjoying her fame from the ongoing Paramount show, Yellowstone. Her role of the feisty Beth Dutton on the three seasons of Yellowstone has made her a household name.

Kelly Reilly's Husband Details

Kelly is one lucky lady when it comes to her love life. She is happily married to her long-time partner.

Kelly met her man, Kyle Baugher in 2010. Interestingly, Kyle is not from the same field as Kelly; he is a financier. Despite their different career paths, the couple hit it off right from the first date and eventually got engaged after a short time. 

Following their engagement, the pair got married in 2012 in a dreamy ceremony held in Somerset, England.

As of now, Kelly and her husband seem to be going pretty strong. Further, from their social media posts, the couple seems to be very much in love with each other.

Before Kelly ended up with her husband, she was rumored to be dating her Sherlock Holmes director Guy Ritchie in 2008. However, both Kelly and Guy denied having anything more than a professional relationship.

Career/ Net Worth

Kelly Reilly was bitten by the acting bug at a very young age. She took drama lessons during her high school and performed in many dramas. 

As a 16-year-old, Kelly wrote a letter to the producers of the TV show Prime Suspect, stating her desire to work in the show. Six months later, she was called for an audition and eventually given the part of Polly Henry in the show.
