Either by saying 'A Girl with the Asian Guy,'' or 'Glegg,' it means to address the on-screen pair of The Walking Dead. Lauren Cohan, the on-screen wife of Steven Yeun, created hype with the TV romance, but the only thing that her fans are concerned about is her real marital life.

So, does she have a concealed dating affair or is she married to someone but hiding the relationship from the eyes of people? Being a fan, you surely want to go through her life happenings and settle your queries related to her. So, let's dig in.

What Is Lauren's Love Status?

The thirty-seven-year-old model turned actress played Maggie Greene, the love interest of Glenn Rhee portrayed by Steven Yeun in the AMC TV series, The Walking Dead. The couple eventually stepped into a marital relationship, after dating for an extended period as girlfriend and boyfriend.

After starting a new chapter as husband and wife, the pair also parented a biological child. But the romance between the couple was limited only to the series.  In the show too, their marriage comes to a tragic end when Glenn gets brutally killed. However, they both are considered a good companion of one another in real life.

Lauren, clarified the same thing earlier in October 2013 as she spoke to Rolling Stone and said:

“We all live within two buildings and five seconds from each other, and we all go to coffee and drinks at the same place, so we always get recognized.”

She further added:

“There are entire websites dedicated to the proof that Steven and I are in a real relationship. Which is always funny, because it's not.”

The former on-screen couple remains friends in their real lives. At present, Lauren is passing her single life, whereas Steven, is in a marital relationship with Joana Pak since December 2016 and they share a baby boy.

Instead, the actress revealed that she has a celebrity crush on an American actor Jeff Bridges. With Maxim, she told: 

“Jeff Bridges. It’s not a romantic crush; it’s just kind of an everything crush. I love him as an actor, I love his voice, I love his smile. OK, now it sounds romantic — but it’s not!”

In 2016, Lauren had dated Lady Gaga's ex-fiance, Christan Carino. The couple was photographed in an event with Hilfiger. Later, they were spotted at a New York Rangers hockey game in 2016.
