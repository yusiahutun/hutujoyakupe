Memphis Depay rewarded himself with a shopping trip to Selfridges in Manchester following his first Premier League goal against Sunderland.

The Manchester United forward was joined by model Karrueche Tran, 27, as he hit the shops on Monday afternoon.

Both Depay, 21, and Tran - the former girlfriend of singer Chris Brown - attended the same hot spots on Saturday as they were pictured outside Suburbia nightclub and Club LIV.

Depay donned fake gold teeth as he hit the city centre to celebrate United's 3-0 league win over the Black Cats. 


The pair were also pictured together during Depay's summer holiday ahead of his first season in England following his £25million move from PSV Eindhoven.
