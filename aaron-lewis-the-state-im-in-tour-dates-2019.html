Aaron Lewis Announces New Album, Acoustic Tour for 2019

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  480 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Aaron Lewis has revealed the cities and dates for a tour that will promote a new album he will release in 2019.

The Staind frontman turned outlaw country singer has announced his The State I'm In Tour, which kicks off Jan. 24 at the Majestic Theatre in Dallas and runs for 24 dates before wrapping up at the Little River Casino in Manistee, Mich., on April 6. The tour will fall in advance of a new album titled The State I'm In that Lewis will release in 2019, and the upcoming shows will feature the singer-songwriter in a solo acoustic setting, performing songs and telling personal stories from the stage.

“My music is very first person, very autobiographical,” Lewis says in a press release. “It’s from my life experiences that I draw inspiration for my songs. This acoustic tour gives me the opportunity to share the stories behind my songs with my fans and play them stripped down, like I wrote them.”

Tickets for Lewis' The State I'm In Tour are set to go on sale on Friday (Dec. 7) at 10AM local time via the singer's website. Fans who purchase a pair of tickets to any one of the upcoming shows will also pre-order The State I'm In, and they'll receive a physical copy of the album when it becomes available via Valory Music Co. next year. So far there are no further details on the project.
