34-year-old Colin Rand Kaepernick, known professionally as Colin Kaepernick, is one of the most prominent figures of the 2010s.

Kaepernick was originally only known as the quarterback of the San Francisco 49ers but gained larger prominence in 2016 when he began to kneel during the national anthem.

Kaepernick stated that he kneeled as a protest against racial inequality, racial oppression, and police brutality.

These protests made Kaepernick a household name, with many people praising his actions and many more denouncing them.

The football player's protests led to a wider protest movement that eventually led to the Black Lives Matter movement.

One of Kaepernick's biggest detractors was former President Donald Trump, who said in September 2017 that the NFL owners should fire players who protested during the national anthem. And they did.

Kaepernick has been a free agent since he was let go after the 2016 season. In November 2017, the player filed a grievance lawsuit against the NFL and its owners.

Kaepernick accused the institution and its owners of colluding to keep him out of the league.

Two years later, in February 2019, the player withdrew his grievance after reaching a confidential settlement with the National Football League.

Who Is Colin Kaepernick's Wife To Be, Nessa Diab? 

Although they are not married or engaged, Colin Kaepernick has been dating Nessa Diab since 2015.

Nessa Diab, who goes by the mononym Nessa, is an American radio and television personality and a television host.

Nessa's family is from Egypt, and she was born to an Egyptian mother and father. She has two brothers.

The Diab family grew up in Southern California, but due to her father's job, they frequently moved between California and Saudi Arabia. 

The television personality went to the University of California, Berkeley. She graduated with a degree in mass communications.

Her interest in working for the radio had already manifested while in college, and to that end, Nessa interned at the San Francisco Bay Area Top 40 station Wild 94.9.

Eventually, Nessa got her show called "The Baydestrian Report," where she covered news in the Bay Area.

However, Nessa's province rose when she started conducting artist and celebrity interviews for Wild 94.9. Her interviews piqued the interest of MTV, which sought her out to be on Girl Code.

Since then, Nessa has hosted various MTV and MTV2 shows and currently hosts "Nessa on Air," which airs during the drive-time shift on the top-rated New York City show Hot 97. 

Nessa's show is the number 1 hip hop show in the afternoons in the 18 to 34-year-old demographic.

Do Colin Kaepernick And Nessa Diab Have A Baby?

Colin Kaepernick and long-time girlfriend Nessa Diab announced the birth of their first child on Sunday, August 28, 2022.

According to the caption Nessa posted, she thought long and hard before sharing the news but decided to do it because she felt proud to call herself mom.

The baby had been born a few weeks before she posted the picture. Nessa went on to thank Kaepernick for being an amazing father and supportive.

Nessa and Kaepernick seem very happy with the birth of their child, and the news was greeted with congratulations and well wishes by both of their fans.
