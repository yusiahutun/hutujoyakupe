Regardless, since parting ways with the footballer, Shakira has been looking more glamorous than ever, clearly dressing for herself in some jaw-dropping outfits – perhaps the sweetest revenge of all? Here are some of our faves from the past few months …

1. The Princess Diana-inspired Cannes dress

First on the list is the strapless black dress with sweetheart neckline that Shakira waltzed onto the red carpet in for the 75th Cannes Film Festival.

Taking style revenge notes from the late Princess Diana, the “Hips Don’t Lie” songstress accessorised the gorgeous black gown by New York-based fashion label Mônot with matching gloves and diamond jewellery.

As fans of historical royal fashion may recall, Lady Di wore a strikingly similar “revenge dress” at the Vanity Fair Gala in 1994 – right after it was revealed that her former husband, now-King Charles, had an affair with the now-Queen Consort Camilla Parker-Bowles.

2. Balenciaga chain-pattern blouse

Shakira also put on a show-stopping display for the music video of her now-viral break-up collaboration song with Argentine producer Bizarrap. The Colombian wore a matching hot-pink bralette and satin trousers, then complemented them with a lime-green shirt with a gold chain print by luxury fashion house Balenciaga.

According to Latin media outlets, the outfit might be another message to Piqué, with the golden chains representing their former relationship.

3. Burberry campaign outfit

When Shakira became the star of Burberry’s glamorous 2022 festive campaign, fans quickly declared her outfit another “revenge dress”. The brand’s stylish campaign video aimed to capture Shakira’s interpretation of the “exhilaration of the night before”, which definitely had young free and single vibes.Shakira looked stunning in a sheer nude gown that featured the iconic Burberry branding. The sparkly see-through piece was covered in silver bead detailing. Per Vogue, Kate Moss’ daughter, Lila, wore the same designer dress to the 2022 Met Gala.

4. Aadnevik for Midnight Muse

Aside from promoting her long-awaited new music and landing several magazine covers, Shakira has also been busy promoting her new fragrance, Midnight Muse amid the break up.

The singer showed off her spectacular figure in an Aadnevik spring/summer 2020 skin-coloured ensemble as she promoted her perfume, which hit shelves last year.

5. Miu Miu see-through situation

Shakira was all about that sweet revenge style as the cover star of Elle’s October 2022 issue.

The “Waka Waka” musician slayed in a slew of stylish outfits, including a see-through Miu Miu dress embellished with large crystals paired with silver platform sandals. Shakira also donned a blush pink Versace gown, a Johanna Ortiz cocktail dress and a black Carolina Herrera strapless number, as seen in the Elle article.
