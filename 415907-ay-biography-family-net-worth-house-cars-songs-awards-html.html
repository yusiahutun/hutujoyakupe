A.Y. biography

A.Y. was born on 5th July 1982 in Mtwara, Tanzania. His real name is Ambwene Allen Yessayah. A.Y.'s family details such as parents, siblings, and upbringing are not known to the public. His educational background details have not been revealed to the public too.

Career

The bongo Flava artist began his music career in 1996 with a group known as S.O.G. After singing with the group for around six years; he decided to embark on a solo career. He released his first solo album, Raha Kamili. The first single song from the album was Raha Tupu.

It was a massive success as it topped charts on radio and TV. The second single titled Machoni kama Watu, featured Lady Jay D, was equally well-received. He released his second album, Hisia Zangu, in 2005.

A.Y.'s wife and children

A.Y. from Tanzania is married to his long-time girlfriend, Rehma Remy Munyana. The duo dated for a few before the engagement in 2017 at the Golden Tulip in Tanzania. They exchanged their wedding vows in 2018.

Within the same year, the singer and his wife welcomed their firstborn child, Aviel Yessayah. He was born on 12th August 2018 at the Medical City Healthcare in Dallas, Texas.

House and cars

In 2018, the Tanzanian singer bought his dream home in Calabasas, California, U.S.A. He had always desired to own a home in the States, and so that was a dream come true for him.
