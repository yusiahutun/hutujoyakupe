The Oldest Professional Football Club in Scotland

Dumbarton FC holds the distinction of being the oldest professional football club in Scotland. Founded in 1872, it has a rich history that spans over a century. The club has seen incredible highs and lows, but its enduring legacy has solidified its place in Scottish football folklore.
