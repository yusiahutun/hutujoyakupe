The Formation of Milton Keynes Dons FC

Milton Keynes Dons FC, formerly known as Wimbledon FC, was formed in 2004 following the controversial relocation of the original Wimbledon FC to Milton Keynes. This move sparked a significant amount of debate and criticism among football fans.

The Stadium: Stadium MK

The home ground of Milton Keynes Dons FC is Stadium MK, which has a seating capacity of over 30,000 spectators. This modern and state-of-the-art stadium provides a fantastic atmosphere for fans to enjoy the games.

League Success

FA Cup Run
