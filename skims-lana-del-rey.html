When Kim Kardashian’s shapewear and intimates brand, SKIMS, previewed its Valentine’s Day collection with a campaign starring Lana Del Rey, the Internet was gagged. How did no one come up with something like this sooner? The partnership made total sense and had many of us itching to shop all the pieces the singer-songwriter was wearing.

Now, everything from the SKIMS Valentine’s Shop is finally available for purchase, and customers are likely to find something that suits their specific style ahead of the holiday. From bright pink pajamas and cozy lingerie to candy underwear and hoodies for your dog, this drop includes quite the range and nothing tops $124.

Del Rey’s SKIMS campaign is essentially a vintage pin-up pictorial with modern-day styling: knee-high boots, big satin bows, elbow-length gloves, and glossy makeup. Shot by British photographer Nadia Lee Cohen, one image in particular features the star sitting in a giant, blue velvet, heart-shaped box, dressed in a coordinating baby blue velvet teddy.

“I’ve been a big fan of SKIMS since the beginning,” Del Rey said in an accompanying press release. “Their collection is so pretty and dreamy, which made collaborating with Nadia Lee Cohen on the creative so fun. I hope you love it as much as I do.”

In other shots, the 38-year-old could be seen rocking sweet pink pieces while posing with cats and channeling a goth aesthetic in a black slip dress as an arrow pierces her heart.

The Internet has long recognized Kardashian’s ability to adapt to the present and recruit cultural icons for SKIMS campaigns. She did it in 2022 when she brought Tyra Banks, Heidi Klum, Alessandra Ambrosio, and Candice Swanepoel together to promote her Fits Everybody line. Last year, the brand also tapped SZA, Cardi B, and Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes and his family for three separate drops.
