At this time last year, Adidas Sambas were impossible to find in stock. The humble indoor soccer shoes had become a staple overnight with the help of celebrities like Bella Hadid and Kendall Jenner, plus buzzy Adidas collaborations with Gucci and Grace Wales Bonner. If you didn't already have a pair, you wanted a pair. Cue the "Sold Out" notices.

The Adidas Samba is still very much an 'It shoe', but there's a refreshing twist: Pairs are actually available and included in a rare sale. Specifically, Amazon Prime Day 2023's best fashion deals, which end tonight at midnight, include savings on Adidas Sambas sneakers.

On the first day of Amazon Prime Day, we spotted the classic black Adidas Samba for just over ten percent off. The deal is still available on July 12, Prime Day's second and final stretch. The sneaker is a modern fashion essential worth shopping on sale for a reason. They were originally designed for sport, but they're one of the most comfortable sneakers to wear day-to-day.

The low-top silhouette and three Adidas stripes are also incredibly versatile, as celebrity street style has proven. Bella Hadid has worn her Adidas Sambas with everything from jeans and a plain T-shirt to a grunge plaid skirt and sweater over the past year. Kaia Gerber is also a noted fan, wearing hers with slouchy jeans and a chore coat.
