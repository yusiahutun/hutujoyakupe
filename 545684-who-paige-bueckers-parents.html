The American athlete started playing basketball at the age of five. She began gaining prominence when she became a point guard for Hopkins High School in late 2016.

She later became Minnesota Gatorade Player of the Year and a finalist for Gatorade National Player of the Year in high school. Paige Bueckers has been the point guard of the UConn Huskies women's basketball team at the University of Connecticut since 2020.
