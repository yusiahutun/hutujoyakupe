It is prudent to know which are the good driving schools in Nairobi that will give you value for your investment. Some institutions do not expedite obtaining a driving license, which might sabotage the purpose for which you pursued the driving course.

While scouting for the best driving schools in Nairobi, you should look into more than just the brand names. The cost they charge and the service they offer should be proportional. The quality of the training should also matter. There are institutions that will charge you 'fairly,' but at the end of it all, you will have wasted your time and money. You should know if they have enough personnel and equipment to give you the best value for your money.

What are some of the famous driving schools in Nairobi?

Looking at the best driving schools in Kenya and their prices, most of them are affordable and have a considerable track record. Here is a list of driving schools you could consider.

1. AA Kenya

  • Physical address: Renaissance Corporate Park, Upper Hill
  • Postal address: P.O Box 40087-00100, Nairobi
  • Email: aak@aakenya.co.ke, customercare@aakenya.co.ke, customeradvocate@aakenya.co.ke
  • Website: www.aakenya.co.ke
  • Phone number: 0709933999, 0709933000

The Automotive Association of Kenya, or AA, is one of Kenya's oldest driving schools, having been established long before the country's independence. This institution is one of the most reputable driving schools not only in Kenya but also throughout East Africa.

The school has over 30 branches in key cities across Kenya, including Nairobi, Kisumu, Mombasa, and Nakuru.

Apart from its various branches in different estates in Nairobi, it is also among the top driving schools in Nairobi CBD, therefore more accessible to learners.

Their intensive programs are focused on both learner drivers and trainee instructors. They cover all areas of driving, including theory, practicals, and laws and regulations. Among the many services available are:

  • Learner driver courses for individuals who have never driven and want to learn driving skills from scratch.
  • Trainee instructor course for instructors who want to be trained in all areas of becoming teachers of driving schools.
  • Motorcycle licensing class provides riders with motorcycle riding skills.
