Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis First Red Carpet in 17 Years

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis are rarely seen together when it comes to red carpet appearances. But that changed last night, when the two attended the Sixth Annual Breakthrough Prize Ceremony in Mount View, California, with Kunis in a dazzling Dolce & Gabbana bustier dress and Kutcher looking sharp in a tux and bowtie.

Getty Images

And they couldn't have looked any cuter.

Getty Images

Later, the pair took the stage side by side and presented an award together. They couldn't have made a better team.

The couple last appeared together at the 2017 Billboard Music Awards, but they skipped the red carpet and were only photographed backstage.

Getty Images

Kutcher and Kunis backstage at the Bllboard Music Awards in 2016

However before that, the last time they really walked the red carpet together was in 2000, years before they started publicly dating. Here they are with That '70s Show costar, Danny Masterson at the premiere of Traffic 17 years ago.

Getty Images

Kunis and Kutcher tied the knot in 2015 and have two children together: three-year-old Wyatt Isabelle and one-year-old Dimitri. After meeting and costarring in That '70s Show, when the actress was still a teenager, the two reconnected and ultimately pursued a romantic relationship.

Other stars who attended the event included Morgan Freeman, Kerry Washington, and Lily Collins.

Though Kunis and Kutcher don't often make red carpet appearances, they have been making guest appearances at crowded events, like Lakers games (plenty of which they're seated courtside), LA Dodgers games, and even Stagecoach, California's country music festival, where they were seen wearing flannels and cowboy hats, fitting right in with the crowd.
