Early life

Lil Mama was born Niatia Jessica Kirkland on October 4, 1989, in Brooklyn, New York. She is the eldest daughter of eight children. Her mother is Tara Kirkland, while his father is Allen Brunner. Lil Mama's younger brother, Arnstar, is an underground rapper and dancer who was a regular on Nick Cannon's Wild 'n Out series on MTV.

The artist was raised in Harlem, New York, from kindergarten to seventh grade. Her childhood was difficult as she experienced financial problems with her family. As a result, the family depended on welfare, making them move countless times between apartments and shelters. Similarly, Lil Mama and her siblings were unstable as they bounced between their father and mother.

The rapper's mother lived in Harlem, while her father lived in Eastern New York. Niatia later moved back to Brooklyn and joined Edward R. Murrow High School from her eighth grade until her senior year. She got her nickname, Lil Mama, for being the oldest daughter of her family. Unfortunately, Lil Mama’s mother died on December 15, 2007, after a 4-year battle with bre*ast cancer.

Music

Despite many hardships, Lil Mama followed her young-age passion for music performance. The artist initially focused on dance but eventually took drama classes at a local school.

From an early age, Niatia was a poetry writer. She then began adding a touch of music to her text works with the help of her father. Having noticed his daughter’s talent, Lil Mama’s father took her to his studio, where she recorded her first song.

At the age of 17, the singer relocated from Brooklyn, New York, to Atlanta to enable her work with the music producer of the rap quartet, Nappy Roots, James Groove Chambers. With James' help, she recorded seven songs. Lil Mama’s breakthrough came with the hit song, Lip Gloss, which she recorded with James Groove Chambers.

The song was released on June 19, 2007, and received positive reviews from critics. It initially debuted at number ninety-five spot on the Billboard Hot 100 Chart. Within a short time, it re-entered the chart at the number twelve spot after its release on iTunes. Finally, Lip Gloss peaked at number 10 spot.
