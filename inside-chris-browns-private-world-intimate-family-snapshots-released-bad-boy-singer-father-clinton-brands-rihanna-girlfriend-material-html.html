The world has become more accustom to seeing Chris Brown ripping off his shirt or attending court appearances.

But before he was R&B's bad boy, he was a chubby-faced baby who loved to dress up and pose for photographs.

Chris's father Clinton Brown has opened up his family album to show the world the boy he knew growing up - while also branding his son's decision to reunite with girlfriend Rihanna a poor choice.

Opening up: Chris Brown's father Clinton Brown has released childhood pictures of his son while branding the singer's decision to reunite with girlfriend Rihanna a poor choice

Speaking from his modest home in Tappahannock, Virginia, Clinton told a number of media outlets he felt the couple were better off apart.

The prison officer - whom split with the singer's mother when Chris was six - told New York's Daily News: 'I personally really didn’t want him and Rihanna back together.'

'You have to have a balance in a relationship.

Back on: Chris Brown and Rihanna have rekindled their stormy relationship, much to the disdain of his father

'You have to have someone who is spontaneous and whimsical but you also have to have someone who is grounded and logical.'

He went on to say he thinks the pair are too similar to maintain a steady relationship and that their union could end in tragedy.

'I hate to use examples, but Michael Jackson, Whitney Houston and Amy Winehouse — I mean I could go on and on.

'Is that a given — that you can’t have fortune and fame without (death) being the end result?'

Before he was famous: One of the pictures shows a five-year-old Chris looking smart in suit and shoes while posing for a family photograph in 1994

He also claims that Chris is now surrounded by a network of 'yes men' and that this too could lead him along the road to ruin.

Thankfully, Clinton says that as he is not on the payroll he can still dish out good advice to his son when needed.

Clinton does show some signs that he's the father of a successful singer, as he drives a brand new silver BMW - courtesy of Chris.

Meanwhile, superstar Chris seems a long way away from the baby boy pictured in his father's scrap book.

Ahoy there sailor: The singer probably wished this picture of him dressed up in a sailor costume had stayed private

Showing promise: As a teen Brown showed a lot of talent on the court

Clinton revealed his favourite snaps of his young son, which includes a host of Christmas photos in addition to some the bad boy rapper might have wished the world had not seen.

They include a five-year-old Chris dressed up in his finest blue suit with a white polo neck shirt, and a snap of a tiny baby Chris wearing a white sailor's outfit complete with a big red bow.

Luckily for the 23-year-old, his father also released some pictures which showed the singer developing a more hip hop look.

Looking more like himself: In 2003 the star turned his school picture into what looked like an album cover donning oversized blue denim shorts, layered blue and white T-shirts and a matching blue and white cap

Close relationship: Despite splitting with Chris mother when the singer was six the pair have remained close. Chris and Clinton are pictured here after one of Chris's shows in Portsmouth, Virginia in 2007

In 2003, the star turned his school picture into what looked like an album cover, donning oversized blue denim shorts, layered blue and white T-shirts and a matching blue and white cap.

As Chris still does today, the 14-year-old accessorised his look with a large gold watch and brand new high top sneakers.

Clinton also hinted that in his opinion, the 2009 row that led to Rihanna being beaten by Chris wasn't one sided.

Christmas Chris: The singer really loved to wear a turtleneck during the festive season

Knowing his angles: The star was photographed in the same pose each year seen here in 1992 and 1994

He said: 'One has to assume because they are back together that it wasn't one-sided.

'You just have to imagine that it was a circumstance in which they both had a disagreement.'

However, the fact that the incident, or more accurately the photograph of Rihanna's bruised and bleeding face after it happened, is still very much at the front of people's minds.

Clinton, 48, says he acknowledges this and thinks that the intense public scrutiny that follows the couple's every move will also make it hard for their love to thrive.

Proud moment: A beaming six-year-old Brown graduates kindergarten

Not a mansion: Clinton lives in a modest home in Tappahannock, Virginia

He revealed he wishes Chris would date a different singer.

'I personally liked Jordin Sparks. She's a wholesome young lady, very pretty. Of course, a lot of the times we don't want the nice, conservative types.'

Clinton also revealed that he was fond of Chris' other girlfriend, Karrueche Tran, who he was reportedly dating at the same time as Rihanna.

He claims that Chris and Karrueche got on 'terrifically', which is a wild contrast to the rocky relationship between his son and his current girlfriend.

Representatives for both Rihanna and Chris Brown did not respond to requests to comment on Clinton's interview when contacted by MailOnline.
