An Italian Aristocrat Don Lorenzo Salviati Is A Descendant Of Lorenzo De’ Medici

Few people know this, but Don Lorenzo Salviati comes from a high-class Italian family. He is an Italian Duke and one of the descendants of the Italian statesman Lorenzo de Medici.

Don Lorenzo Salviati was born on 10 March 1957 in Rome, Italy, to Don Foroze Salviati and his mother Maria Grazia Gavronska. His father is the 5th Duke of Don Lorenzo Salviati, Marquis of Montieri, and Boceggiano, Nobile Romano Coscritto.

He is survived by two sisters, Donna Maria Oliva and Donna Dianora.

As regards his education, Lorenzo is reported to have studied business at the University of Southern California.

Don Lorenzo Salviati Was Married To an Actress

However, their cruise marriage, which was supposed to be a romantic event, turned into an adventure.
