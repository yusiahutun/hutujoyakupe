Self-proclaimed richest Kenyan singer Akothee has jokingly said she would get married to another Mzungu soon.

Has Akothee parted ways with her Mzungu man?

Akothee took to social media to share that she was healing after going through a lot for the past few months.

The mother of five got married to Denis Schweizer, aka Omosh, in a glamorous wedding in April and were to hold their second wedding in July in Switzerland, but it didn't happen.

Akothee disclosed all was not well after discovering "the truth and ugly stuff." She said she broke down several times while going through the dark time, thanking her family and close friends for being there for her.

"I have come out of a very dangerous zone, and I have been healing in private. I am in my second month of therapy. The trauma I went through after finding out the truth and some ugly stuff left me shaking. I went for days without food or sleep, questioning and answering myself.

It has been very heavy some days; a night will just break into a day with me looking outside the window, gazing at nothing. I would be shaking for reasons I can't explain..." she wrote in part.

Reacting to Akothee's heartbreaking post, comedian-turned-radio host Dr Ofweneke, who emceed at the singer's wedding, shared a video from the glamorous event.

He jokingly told Akothee that he would still emcee at her wedding if she remarried, encouraging her that all would be well.

"Hata ufanye harusi ngapi (It doesn't matter how many weddings you will do), I will always be here. Leta Mzungu mwingine. Lakini lazima nicheke (Get another Mzungu and wed him) . This one, too, shall come to pass; you will be okay," he said.

Responding to Ofweneke's post, Akothee jokingly said she would remarry.

"Ochise the 8th loading ."
