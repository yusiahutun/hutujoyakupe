Uganda - A happily married woman has shared her awe-inspiring love story with her amazing Chinese hubby.

Ugandan lady speaking fluent Chinese language

During a Q and A on YouTube, the gorgeous lady from Uganda narrated how she connected with her loving husband.

Rose Mary Adikin, who creates food content with her hubby Wu Jianyun on TikTok, could not hide her fetching smile when the question popped up.

"Back then, my husband's cousin worked with me in Uganda," she gladly responded in fluent Chinese.

Sourcing contact from cousin

Her husband Wu, who comfortably sat close to Adikin with a cap and hands on the table, took over from her response.

"She asked me, 'How about I introduce you to a foreigner? Would that be okay?' I said, 'Okay,'" he simply stated as he looked at the love of his life.

Adikin continued saying Wu got her number from his cousin, which was the genesis of their chatting.

Boarding aeroplane to China

After a while, Adikin said Wu organised for her an aeroplane ticket to visit him in the East Asian country.

"When we saw each other, we had a bit of, 'I liked him, he liked me'. Then we went to get our marriage certificate. We got it in 2015, and we have been together ever since," Adikin narrated.
