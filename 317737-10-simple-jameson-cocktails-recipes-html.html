1. Jameson, ginger, and lime

Among the many Jameson whiskey cocktails, this is by far the most popular. It is even recommended on the brand’s website. This is a blend of three refreshing elements that bring out the best in each other and will easily remind you of a Jameson and lemonade cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 50 ml of Jameson Irish whiskey.
  • Ginger ale.
  • A large wedge of lime.

How to make it

  • In your high glass ball, fill it with ice.
  • Add a shot of Jameson.
  • Pour your high-quality ginger ale.
  • Briefly stir it.
  • Garnish it with a large wedge of lime; slightly squeeze it before dripping it into the glass.

The refreshing taste of Jameson and ginger ale will blow your mind away. It is among the easy Jameson cocktails to make in a DIY process.

2. Irish car bomb

For sure, it is an explosive cocktail, but don't let the name shock you. The result is pure niceness and is something you can look forward to in your Friday sundowner. This cocktail is traditionally associated with beer but can work well with Irish whiskey.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pint Guinness stout.
  • 1/2 shot Bailey’s Irish Cream.
  • 1/2 shot Jameson Irish Whiskey.

How to make it

  • Put Bailey's Irish cream into a shot glass.
  • Add your Jameson Irish whiskey.
  • Pour the Guinness into a beer mug and allow the foam to settle.
  • Drop the shot glass into the Guinness.
  • Devour it fast.

You’ve heard it – drink it fast. It curdles rather quickly, and it will not be appealing at all if it does.

3. Jameson whiskey sour

If you are still wondering how to drink Jameson, then why not add some sour taste, and get a newer perspective to this classic drink.

Ingredients

  • 60 ml of Jameson Irish whiskey.
  • 1 splash sparkling water.
  • 1 bar spoon of granulated sugar.
  • 4 dashes of freshly-squeezed lemon juice.

How to make it

  • Pour lemon juice into the mixing glass.
  • Add sugar and let it dissolve.
  • Add sparkling water into the mixing glass.
  • Pour the whiskey and fill with ice.
  • Mix it well.
  • Strain into a sour glass.
  • Garnish with a cherry.

This is a glorious Jameson old fashioned cocktail that has been a favorite since it was first made two decades ago.

4. Hot apple toddy

Looking for an alternative for the classic mulled wine? This Jameson cider recipe is a great option if you are looking for something interesting. A single glass will enlighten your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • One teaspoon of honey.
  • 2oz Whiskey.
  • 5oz Apple Cider.
  • 1tsp Cloves.

How to make it

  • Pre-heat an Irish coffee glass.
  • Coat the base of the Irish coffee glass with honey.
  • Pour into the coffee glass two ounces of Jameson Irish whiskey.
  • Add hot apple cider.
  • Stir it well.
  • Garnish it with a cinnamon stick, lemon wedge, and a few whole cloves.

5. Irish coffee

If you enjoy a cup of coffee quite often, there’s nothing more quintessential than adding a few ounces of Jameson Irish whiskey. Why take Jameson on the rocks when you have this fantastic alternative?

Ingredients

  • 1/3 parts Demerara and Muscovado sugar syrup.
  • 1 part Jameson.
  • 1 part lightly whipped double cream.
  • 3 parts medium roasted espresso coffee.
  • 1 vanilla pod or cinnamon for garnish.
  • Freshly grated nutmeg.

How to make it

  • Dissolve two parts Demerara sugar and 1 part Muscovado sugar in 3 parts of boiling water to make a sugar syrup.
  • Stir your sugar syrup into 35 ml of Jameson.
  • Whip 30 ml of cream (optional, you can use canned cream to make it easier).
  • Brew 90 ml of espresso coffee (two espresso pours) and add to your whiskey.
  • Float the whipped cream on top.
  • Garnish with a vanilla pod or a stick of cinnamon.

6. Irish gold cocktail

This is one of the best traditional Irish cocktails that go well with your Jameson drink. It is a great way to enjoy the lighter side of Irish whiskey in a simple mix that has a fruity twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of Irish whiskey (Tullamore Dew).
  • 1/2 ounce peach schnapps.
  • Splash orange juice.
  • 4 ounces ginger ale (or enough to fill).
  • Garnish lime wheel.

How to make it

  • Build the whiskey, schnapps, and juice in a Collins glass filled with ice.
  • Top with ginger ale. Garnish with a lime wheel.
  • Serve and enjoy!

It is recommended that you use fresh-squeezed orange juice or a better drink. The strength will rely on how much ginger ale you add. Generally, it will be a mild cocktail comparable to other highballs.

7. Irish mule recipe

It is an exciting spin to the Moscow mule, full of a refreshing lime-ginger flavour.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Jameson whiskey.
  • 1 oz. Rose’s lime juice.
  • 1 fresh lime wedge.
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary – optional.
  • 6–8 oz. ginger beer.

How to make it

  • Muddle the whiskey, lime juice, and rosemary together.
  • Pour into a copper mug or glass with ice.
  • Top with ginger beer, stir and serve
  • It is one of the simplest Jameson cocktails to make and doesn’t require a lot of skills.

8. Jameson and coke

Don't be afraid to test it out with your favorite soda brand. In this classic combo, you will surely enjoy a subtle and sweeter taste. There is a smoothness that is unrivaled when mixed with coke, as it doesn't become overly sweet.

Ingredients

  • 60 ml of Jameson whiskey.
  • Coca Cola.
  • Ice cubes.

How to make it

  • Pour ice into a tall glass to the brim.
  • Pour 60 ml of Jameson whiskey.
  • Add 120 ml of Coca Cola or one that is more reliant on your flavour desires.
  • Gently mix.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge (optional)

9. Blackberry malt cocktail

This is a unique Jameson whiskey cocktail that you ought to try. It is ideal for spring and summer and can work for any occasion, even your office party.

Ingredients

  • 4–5 blackberries (fresh).
  • 3/4 ounce simple syrup.
  • 1 3/4 ounces Irish whiskey.
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice (fresh).
  • 3 1/2 ounces sparkling water (or enough to fill the glass).
  • Garnish with lemon peel.

How to make it

  • Pour simple syrup in a cocktail and muddle the blackberries.
  • Pour whiskey and wine and shake it well with ice.
  • Double strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  • Top it up with sparkling water.

10. Irish winter

This is a blend of the monumental Irish coffee Jameson cocktail. It is quite simple to make, such that you can be mixing it regularly.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Jameson Irish whiskey.
  • 3/4 ounce coffee liqueur.
  • 3/4 ounce Irish cream liqueur.
  • 4 ounces coffee.
  • Whipped cream.

How to make it

  • Pour your coffee, Jameson whiskey, and Irish cream into a warm mug.
  • Fill it with hot coffee.
  • Mix it by stirring.
  • Top it up with whipped cream.

It is better to serve this cocktail in a heated glass to sustain its warmth until you get to the last drop.
