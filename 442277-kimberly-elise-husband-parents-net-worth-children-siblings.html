Kimberly Elise's biography

Kimberly Elise Trammel was born on 17th April 1967 in Minneapolis, Minnesota, in the United States. She is 54 years old as of 2022. She holds an American nationality and a Caucasian-white ethnic background.

Her parents are Erma Jean Trammel (mother) and Marvin Trammel (father). Kimberly Elise's mother was a teacher at a local elementary school, while her father owned an executive search firm. She was raised alongside her three siblings, two elder twin brothers, and one younger sister.

After her elementary and high school studies, she joined The American Film Institute for a Directing course. Later, she enrolled at the University of Minnesota for a Bachelor of Arts degree in Mass Communication.

Career

Kimberly began her film career in 1955 after starring in two movies, Newton's Apple as Bile Duct Supervisor and In the House as Roulette. The following year, she starred Candace Blake in The Sentinel. She made her big-screen debut in 1996 when she landed the role of Tisean Williams in Set It Off.

Kimberly Elise's husband and children

Is Kimberly Elise married? No. The actress is currently single after divorcing her long-term husband. She was previously married to Maurice Oldham. Maurice was an American photographer born on 30th December 1958 in Princeton, New Jersey, United States.

The couple tied the knot in 1989 and ended the union in divorce in 2005 after being married for 16 years. They were blessed with two daughters AjaBleu Arial Oldham (born on 16th March 1990) and Butterfly Rose Oldham (born on 19th October 1998). He passed away in 2007 after succumbing to a massive blood clot.
