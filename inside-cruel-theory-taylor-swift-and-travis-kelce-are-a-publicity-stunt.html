Inside cruel theory Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are a 'publicity stunt'

 Posted on September 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been sending fans, and the paps wild, ever since they first sparked dating rumors in September. However, with publicity always comes negative talks, as some people called their relationship a ‘publicity stunt’ for the NFL player. Now, these theories are being shut down once and for all.

In September, Taylor Swift was pictured attending a Kansas City Chiefs Game, and ever since then she’s been the supportive girlfriend, attending all the games she can. Naturally, the celebrity couple has been gaining attention, and now the Chiefs CEO has spoken out.

Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s relationship is ‘purely organic,’ says Chiefs CEO

Clark Hunt, CEO of the Kansas City Chiefs has spoken out about the relationship that is the talk of the globe, and possibly, the NFL field. He told CNBC’s Jim Cramer their relationship is “purely organic”.

“When they were just starting off dating, there were a lot of people saying, ‘Oh, it’s a marketing stunt.’ But no, it’s very real,” he said. As well as the Swifties, the Chiefs are also supporting the relationship, as Hunt added: “And as I said, the most important thing is we’re happy for the two of them.”

The CEO then went on to call the music star “as sweet as she can be,” and “one of the most amazing artists of our time.”

But, he didn’t deny the relationship had grown the Chiefs fan base, especially the female audience. “Our female audience has grown leaps and bounds,” he revealed, calling it “a very interesting, very fun year.”

The couple sent fans wild at the latest game, as the NFL star was seen throwing a ‘heart’ gesture at his girlfriend, after scoring his epic touchdown.

The win means Kelce and the team are just one more win away from the big Super Bowl, however, the Cruel Summer star may not be in attendance if they do make it. The big event will be held on Sunday, February 11 in Vegas, with Taylor heading to Tokyo for her Eras shows from the 7th-10th, so it looks like time may not allow it.

Taylor tips stadium worker $100

It’s not just the Chiefs CEO saying nice words about the star. As well as Travis’ dad, and brother Jason Kelce, stadium workers have nice things to say about the singer, who continues tipping them $100.

A picture of the star handing out the bills in Massachusetts went viral at the end of last year, and the same thing happened at the Bills game over the weekend.

One Highmark Stadium worker, Jerris Rainey, told 7 News Buffalo: “I just wanted to just see her, and she, you know she stopped, and she asked me if I worked at the stadium.”

“I said yes, and she asked if she could give me a tip. She gave me $100 and then she asked if I wanted to take a picture with her and I said yes.”

Talk about a good day at work!
