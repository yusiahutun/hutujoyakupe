The two match in metallic for the 65th Annual Grammy Awards, bringing an extra dose of glamour to their signature edgy style. Fox wears an ivory gown with a sheer corset bodice adorned with sparkling silver and gold embellishments, accessorizing with white Santoni heels and Lorraine Schwartz jewelry. June 29, 2022

The pair continue their hot-pink streak for the NYC after-party following Kelly’s show at Madison Square Garden. For the outing, Fox wears a metallic fuchsia set from the Andamane, consisting of a crop top and a ruched midi skirt, paired with metallic-pink accessories, including strappy lace-up sandals from Femme and a micro crossbody bag with a crystal top handle from L’alingi. “Life in Pink” Premiere (June 27, 2022)

Arturo Holmes//Getty Images

The two put an edgy spin on the Barbiecore fashion trend at the premiere of the rapper’s Hulu documentary, Life in Pink. On the carpet, the actress opts for pink-on-pink-on-pink, debuting platinum-pink hair alongside a two-toned minidress with a keyhole cutout from designer Nensi Dojaka. May 15, 2022

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The pair live up to their edgy-goth reputation with coordinating black ensembles at the 2022 Billboard Music Awards. The Jennifer’s Body actress looks ravishing in an elegant black David Koma gown featuring a dramatic V-cut and a pelvic-grazing leg slit—paired with the London-based designer’s famous crystal-embroidered floral gloves, which nearly reached her shoulders. “Good Mourning” Premiere

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

The two elevate the Barbiecore trend during their joint red-carpet appearance at the premiere of Good Mourning, their film with Pete Davidson. Fox wears a bedazzled pink gown custom-made by Philosophy di Lorenzo Serafini that features a sweetheart neckline and hip-high slit; she doubles down on the glamour with a slick, wavy hairstyle. MGK opts for a hot-pink silk suit with a red-rose print, topped with pink-tinted hair.

10

September 12, 2021

(C)Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Fox turns heads at the 2021 MTV Video Music Awards with a sultry look featuring a sparkling sheer Mugler gown that reveals only a silver thong underneath. She wears it with a curly wet hairstyle. Kelly, meanwhile, poses beside her on the carpet in a glittery red pinstriped suit and silver facial jewelry.

In a viral red-carpet interview from the evening, Fox says her nude sheer look was inspired by the rapper’s insistence that she look “naked” that night. She cheekily told Entertainment Tonight, “I was like, ‘Whatever you say, daddy!’ Whatever daddy says.”

14

September 24, 2020

MEGA//Getty Images

The newly confirmed couple embrace emo fashion for a date night in Los Angeles. MGK wears a white vest top and jeans, featuring blotches of pink and graffiti illustrations that spell out “No Safety.” Walking beside Kelly, Fox wears an all-black fit featuring skinny black jeans and a black leather vest.
