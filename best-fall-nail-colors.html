The 10 Best Fall Nail Polish Colors

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  685 words  |  Larita Shotwell

In my humble and honest opinion, fall is the best season.

And before you start debating me, just hear me out: There's no humidity, which means you sweat less, hot coffee is once again seasonally appropriate, and wearing a thick, cozy sweater is honestly the next best thing to being wrapped up in a duvet.

Another reason why I love fall: The onset of sweater weather means changing my nail polish colors, too. Suddenly all of those neons and pastels I've been wearing during the summer don't feel right. Instead, I always find myself reaching for warm or muted colors because they have more of a fall vibe.

To get inspiration for my fall 2022 manicures, I turned to top nail artists to get the details on what shades are going to be trending this season.

Here, the 10 best nail polish colors to try this fall.

Rusty Red: Sundays Studio Nail Polish in No.24

Why not look to the changing leaves for manicure inspiration? This warm, rusty red is right on theme. "These shades remind us of the leaves changing, and are energetic and uplifting, says Amy Ling Lin, CEO and Founder of Sundays Studio.

Shimmery Copper: ORLY Nail Lacquer in Stop the Clock

You don't need to be skilled at nail art to elevate your manicures. A metallic polish like this one from ORLY is guaranteed to earn compliments. "This rusty, copper shade has a slight shimmer to it to catch the eye," says Sonya Belakhlef, celebrity nail artist.

Cool Gray: Essie Nail Polish in ChinChilly

Beige and baby pink aren't the only neutral manicure options. For anyone who's looking to switch up their minimalist manicures this fall, this mauve-gray is the shade to try. Nail artist Hang Nguyen is a fan of Essie's Nail Polish in Chinchilly.

Olive Green: CND Vinylux in Olive Grove

If you're in the market for a transitional nail polish shade that matches the transitional fall weather, look no further than olive green. "The deep green shade is one of my favorites because it reminds me of the leaves changing colors."

Mocha Brown: Zara Nail Polish in Power

Like soft scarves and pumpkin spice lattes, a deep brown manicure goes hand-in-hand with fall. "This is a great true-brown fall color," says Nguyen.

Soft Purple: Sundays Studio Nail Polish in No.27

If you're one of the many people who have been gallivanting around Europe this summer, a soft purple manicure will recenter you ahead of the new season. "Many of us have been traveling this summer. This fall we need a shade like No.27 to keep us feeling relaxed and grounded," says Lin.
