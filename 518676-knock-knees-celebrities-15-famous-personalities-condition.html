Knock knees causes

According to the National Health Service, knock knees are a normal part of growth and development and get better once someone gets older. However, it can be caused by something else in children and adults, such as a bone infection, arthritis, Vitamin D deficiency, genetic condition or injury.

Can knock knees be corrected?

You can undergo osteotomy surgery to correct severe deformities or knock knees that do not get better on their own or by the time the child has finished growing. The primary aim of the procedure is to straighten the legs and change the angle of the bones.
