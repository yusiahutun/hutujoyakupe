Jennifer Lopez Went House-Hunting in Platform Heels

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  278 words  |  Aldo Pusey

When it comes to walking around New York City, comfort is key. And this would seem to be especially true when touring multiple, multi-million dollar homes all day long. But Jennifer Lopez proved that no number of flights of stairs, or amount of square-footage, will ever get in the way of her and her beloved platforms. 

On Saturday, the singer-slash-actress was spotted house-hunting in the Big Apple with her husband, Ben Affleck, and rather than wear sneakers or flats, she slipped on her tallest heels for the occasion. As a testament to her endurance, J.Lo opted for a pair of nude peep-toe pumps that lifted her several inches off the ground. She teamed her fancy footwear with wide-leg jeans with frayed hems and a camel-colored turtleneck sweater with black stripes that matched her double-breasted wool coat. Lopez carried a glossy black Birkin bag and finished accessorizing with brown-tinted sunglasses and giant diamond stud earrings.  

Her caramel-highlighted hair was swept up into a top knot, and she rounded out her glam with a bronzy contour and glossy lips. 

Affleck, meanwhile, kept it casual in a Nirvana T-shirt layered underneath a plaid flannel shirt and a navy blue jacket. Black jeans, aviator sunglasses, and Nike sneakers provided the final touches to his relaxed weekend look. 

The previous day, J.Lo and Affleck were photographed out and about for another stylish stroll — and the "On the Floor Singer" decided against comfy shoes yet again. This time around, she wore a pair of pointy-toe stiletto boots in a deep shade of Bordeaux that perfectly matched her croc-embossed Birkin bag.

J.Lo is anything if not consistent.
