Already a Superstar as a Child!

From the age of twelve, Conwell showed the signs of being a superstar. She began modeling and competing in beauty pageants from a very early age. She made her first appearance in the TV series, “Dave’s World” from 1994 to 1995 and “Renegade” in 1995.

The work follows other recurring roles as Kianna in 2001 in the TV series “City Guys.” She was further included in the TV series, “For Richer or Poorer” from 2012 to 2013. She starred as Lisa Stallworth in the series, “Family Time” along with her ex-boyfriend, Omar Gooding.

She had played a significant role in the movies like “Baby Boy” and “The Wash.” Among her works, her best-known roles are Leslie in the TV soap opera, “The Young and the Restless” and movie, “Baby Boy.”

Awards and Accolades:

Her most acclaimed work in “Baby Boy” was honored with a special mention at the Locarno International Film Festival in 2001. Her career got escalated from her work in the movie.

She was further nominated for Image Awards for Outstanding Actress in a Daytime Drama Series, “The Young and the Restless” in 2013 and 2014.
