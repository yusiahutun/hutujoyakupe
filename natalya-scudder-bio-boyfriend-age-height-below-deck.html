Meet Natalya Scudder, Stew On Below Deck Mediterranean

Natalya Scudder is one of the new faces in Below Deck Mediterranean season 7 which premiered on 11 July after nearly nine months since the last season. This season viewers will head to Malta. A new yacht named Home is the home of seven newbies. She is working as a third stew who is starring alongside other new casts Jason Gaskell (Deckhand), Storm Smith (Deckhand), Raygan Tyler (Bosun), Kyle Viljoen (second Stew), Dave White (Chef), and Natasha Webb (Chief Stew).

The returning members are Mzi “Zee” Dempers and Captain Sandy. The show revolves are a similar theme to the first six seasons that came before i.e. what it is like to work on a yacht and drama that sprouts when the crew members start working together. It is presently in its seventh season and has shown great growth in popularity since making its debut in 2016.

Some viewers might mistake her for Natasha Webb as they are almost a spitting image of one another. During the season 7 trailer, Natalya told viewers: “Is this my sister? Is this my twin?”. It also seems that Natalya could be bringing us the drama this year, as she hinted at a potential Below Deck lover.

Before the show premiered, Natalya announced on her Instagram, “Surprise! Don’t miss me on #BelowDeckMed Monday, July 11th on @BravoTV! For the full trailer, head to BravoTV.com”.

At age 18, Natalya landed her first yachting position after establishing her skills and achieving the seemingly unattainable. She has since served as a Second Stew and Chief Stew on a number of various boats. She has served affluent clients, including several royal families, and no guest request has ever been too bizarre for her.

Natalya gained a distinctive and alarming perspective on the environmental problems facing the marine industry while working as a stewardess aboard the 55-meter Step One. She spoke with Boat International at the 2017 Monaco Yacht Show about her realization that single-use plastics can have a big detrimental impact on the ecosystem.

Natalya explained, “Last year when we were based in Costa Rica we had an incident where there was a turtle floating next to the yacht. It was just floating on the surface of the water so we decided to pull it out and we called the vet. It turns out that the turtle had swallowed a plastic bag”. She is committed to her work while still managing to be hilarious, whether she’s entertaining the guests or creating gorgeous tablescapes. She is also registered at Crew HQ.

Besides her work in the yachting world, she is also striving to become an influencer. In February 2022 promoted @the_secret_skincare products.
