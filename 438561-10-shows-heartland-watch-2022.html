If you are looking for something to watch after Heartland, then Yellowstone is the best alternative. It is an American television series created by Taylor Sheridan and John Linson. It premiered on June 20, 2018, on the Paramount Network.

The popular television series stars Wes Bentley, Kevin Costner, Kelly Reilly, Luke Grimes, Cole Hauser and Gill Birmingham. Yellowstone follows the Dutton family, who controls the largest contiguous ranch in the United States of American under constant attack by those it borders.

Yellowstone's fourth series premiered on November 7, 2021, and has aired eight episodes so far (until December 19, 2021). The television series exposes the struggles that people running ranches undergo and the violence and corruption in their world.

John Dutton (Kevin Costner) will do everything in his power to keep his land from the greedy fingers of the government, developers and indigenous tribes that lay claim to it. However, the real enemies seem to come from within, his family members who seem to have aspirations.
