Quaid, who portrayed singer Jerry Lee Lewis in the 1989 movie Great Balls of Fire, has had longtime ties to music, as he's the singer and guitarist in his band, The Sharks.

'Music really got me through life,' he told the outlet last year. 'It always has. Music is like therapy. I've been writing songs, and now I have a journal of my entire life through songs that I've written through the decades.'

The Hollywood stalwart, famed for films such as Traffic, Everybody's All-American, Any Given Sunday and American Underdog is gearing up to star in another biopic, Reagan, in the lead role of 40th President Ronald Reagan in the movie Reagan.
