CHRIS Pine's fans have been suspecting the star got Botox and fillers as he makes his debut at the Don't Worry Darling movie premiere.

Fans are shocked at the star's sudden transformation.

Chris, 42, stars in the psychological thriller movie that made its debut at the Venice Film Festival over this past week.

Although the star-studded film has many fans buzzing, others are more concerned about Chris' recent appearance.

As photos from Chris' red carpet look went viral on the internet, fans all began saying the same thing.

Fans couldn't help but notice Chris' face appeared fuller and puffier than usual.

HAIR RAISING

This isn't the first time fans have been in awe of the A-list actor's transformation.

Earlier this summer, fans had trouble recognizing the star as he was directing his first movie on set.

Chris debuted long beach blonde hair and a growing scruffy grey beard on the set of Poolman.

He accessorized with a straw hat as he looked smart in grey slack trousers and a striped shirt.

He completed his look with white shoes and a camera round his neck as he posed amongst vintage cars on the film set.

Poolman was shooting in Los Angeles at the time.

Chris acted, wrote, and directed the mystery-comedy film.

PINING FOR A CHANGE

In winter of this year, fans were thrown off about the star's identity again because of his grey beard.

Chris was spotted out for a quick coffee run in a grey sweatsuit and black jogger leggings.

His outfit was complete with easy slide-on white Birkenstocks.

TREKKING THROUGH HIS DATING HISTORY

The Star Trek actor has allegedly dated A-listers from Zoe Kravitz to Olivia Munn, who welcomed son Malcolm Hiệp Mulaney with comedian John Mulaney in November of 2021.

Olivia allegedly dated Chris for five months in 2009.

The pair were said to have called it quits in January 2010.

Chris has been dating girlfriend Annabelle Wallis since 2018.

The Hills: New Beginnings star Audrina Patridge revealed on the Hollywood Raw podcast with Dax Holt and Adam Glyn the she and Chris also dated in 2009 but split because he wasn’t interested in her “clubbing” scene.

Audrina said their “lives were completely different” and they couldn’t make it work, though they did share an interesting kiss that she’ll “never forget.” 

SPONTANEOUS SQUID SMOOCH

Audrina said on their 2009 fling: “So, being on The Hills, our lives were completely different, so opposite.

“He was more of a real actor, and theater actor, and liked reading books and jazz music, didn’t really like to go out to clubs or anything like that. 

“And, at that time in my life, that was all I was doing, going out to clubs because we were filming all the time and being around people.”

Despite it not working out, Audrina gushed about how Chris was a “nice guy, very charming, and a gentleman.”

During an Italian dinner date, they shared a very sweet, spontaneous and squid-involving kiss.

Chris ordered black squid pasta, and she “didn’t really know what that was” at the time - his lips got completely black while eating it.
