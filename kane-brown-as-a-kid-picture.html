This Adorable Pic of a Tiny Kane Brown Proves He Hasn't Changed

 Posted on March 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  224 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kane Brown shared with fans a pic from his childhood that proves he has always been downright adorable.

"I miss that jacket," he notes in a tweet alongside a picture of a much younger Kane Brown.

He hasn't changed much. Because while Brown’s devoted fans love him for his voice, his songs and his onstage presence, they've also fallen for that mischievous grin that he flashes to the crowd as much as he can. And yes, that’s the same grin that can be seen in that faded pic from years ago.

He has much to grin about these days — not only is Brown still riding high on the success of radio singles "Heaven" and "Used to Love You Sober," the double 2018 ACM Awards nominee (New Male Vocalist, Vocal Event of the Year alongside Lauren Alaina for their duet "What Ifs") recently received the news that the Recording Industry Association of America gave a platinum title to both his self-titled debut album and "Heaven." Both reached one million units of total physical sales, downloads and on-demand audio and/or video stream equivalents.

And yes, that’s a mouthful. But basically, it means that Brown is reaching his childhood dreams — dreams that that kid with the multi-color jacket may never have dreamt he could make come true.
