TALK OF THE TOWN: Ellie Goulding reveals she had to get christened before wedding to Caspar Jopling

 Posted on February 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ellie Goulding and Caspar Jopling on their wedding day

Ellie Goulding has revealed there was a small problem to overcome before her lavish wedding to Old Etonian Caspar Jopling could go ahead at York Minster last summer – she had to get christened first. 

'I was never brought up with any particular religion, whereas my husband was,' says the pop star on her big day. 'So I did get christened so I could get married.'

Ellie – who was linked to Prince Harry in 2016 – also admitted her love life was a bit of a mess before she met Caspar, a former Sotheby's art dealer. 

'I kissed quite a few frogs. That sounds harsh but that's the best way to say it,' she jokes. 'I did have a few relationships before Caspar... that weren't what I thought they were.

'But when I met Caspar, I just knew.'

I never had Harry down as a frog…
