Billie Eilish is letting fans get closer and closer to her, from adding us to her Close Friends Story to showing off her stomach tattoo (in her underwear), we’re basically all besties now.

The music star has been sending fans wild lately, as her Instagram followers were in for the shock of their lives when they saw that all-important green circle around her name. Had Billie Eilish added us personally to her Close Friends list? Shortly after we found out all her followers had made the cut, but it still counts right? For those of us who didn’t get a look at the ‘intimate’ story, don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

Billie Eilish shows off stomach tattoo as she poses in her underwear

Earlier in the week, the 22-year-old gave her ‘close friends’ a sneak peek of her newest hip/stomach tat, which reads: Hard & Soft.

The star posed in a black T-shirt, which she lifted up so fans could see the chic artwork, which was placed above her grey boxers. The Bad Guy singer certainly wasn’t shy of showing us her underwear!

Billie’s ‘reveal’ comes months after fans were treated with a glimpse of the first half at the Variety Hitmakers red carpet in December, where the word ‘Hard’, could be seen peaking out over her low-rise jeans.

It’s not the first tattoo the Grammy winner has shocked fans with. In October, the singer showed off her huge back tattoo, traveling all the way down her back.

She once revealed on an Instagram Q&A, that her favorite tattoo was her “sweet little guardian angel fairies” on her hand, but that may have changed with her new inkings. Fans definitely seem to love this one.

“If i had to name a celeb crush, it’d be her,” said one fan on X, formerly known as Twitter.

“SHE’S BREAKING THE INTERNET” exclaimed another.

A third penned: “SHES GOING ALL IN ON THIS ERA. IM HERE FOR IT.”

The star gained over 7 million followers from her ‘clever’ tactic

It looks like everyone wants to be in Eilish’s green circle, as the clever marketing technique has gained her over 7 million followers. Not bad!

According to CrowdTangle, (via Billboard), the star gained over 7 million during a two-day span of adding her 100-million-plus followers to her “Close Friends” Story, where she’s been cryptically teasing her new album.

As well as the online promo, the singer’s been placing billboards in cities such as New York, Los Angeles and London matching the aesthetics, which fans believe are lyrics to some new tracks.

If this album is half as good as the promo, we’re all in for a big treat!
