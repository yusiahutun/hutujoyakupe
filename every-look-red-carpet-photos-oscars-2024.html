Every Celebrity Red-Carpet Look at the 2024 Oscars

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  164 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The 2024 Academy Awards are finally here.

Celebrating the very best in film, the annual award show also presents another opportunity for our favorite stars to show off their red-carpet best.

In the past, the Oscars have been a place where celebrities go big (and bold) with their fashion, from Björk’s swan dress to Celine Dion’s backward Dior tuxedo. And tonight, with stylish nominees like Emma Stone, Margot Robbie, America Ferrera, Carey Mulligan, and Da’Vine Joy Randolph set to make an appearance, we have no doubt it will be an evening of looks to remember.

The 96th Academy Awards ceremony is taking place tonight at Hollywood’s Dolby Theatre, with late-night star Jimmy Kimmel as host. Major celebrities will also be joining Kimmel onstage as presenters, including Jennifer Lawrence, Michelle Yeoh, Ke Huy Quan, Jamie Lee Curtis, Rita Moreno, and Nicolas Cage.
