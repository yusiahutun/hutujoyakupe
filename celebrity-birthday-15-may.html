Celebrity Birthdays 15 May: Zara Tindall, Andy Murray and Stella Maxwell

 Posted on September 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  364 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

We are wishing a very happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating their day of birth today – 15 May. At HELLO!, we are taking a look at some of the famous faces who will be enjoying their birthdays with their loved ones - and plenty of cake and champagne hopefully! From a Hollywood actress to a very popular presenter in the UK, take a look to see who's celebrating today…

Zara Tindall turns 37

Happy Birthday to Zara Tindall! It is an exciting time for the Queen's granddaughter, who is preparing to welcome her second child with rugby-player husband Mike Tindall. In a week of celebrations for Zara, she will be attending her cousin Prince Harry's wedding on Saturday, where her four-year-old daughter Mia is rumoured to be a flower girl. Mike, who has known the Prince for nearly 20 years, recently said of Harry and Meghan: "There is a huge wave of support from people wanting them to be happy and successful. Zara and I can only echo that. The cousins are very close."

Andy Murray turns 31

Today is tennis ace Andy Murray's birthday! Birthday celebrations are likely to be a family affair for the two-time Wimbledon champion, who shares two-year-old daughter Sophia and a 6-month-old baby girl with wife Kim Sears. Andy is hoping to return to the famous grass tournament this year after undergoing a hip operation in January to treat a chronic hip problem. His mum Judy recently told the BBC, "His goal was always to be ready for the grass-court season and, fingers crossed, that will happen."

Stella Maxwell turns 28

Victoria's Secret model Stella turns 28 today! It has been a fun-filled few weeks for Stella in the run-up to her birthday – she attended the Met Gala last week, embracing the heavenly bodies theme in a stunning Moschino gown. Stella, who is currently dating Twilight actress Kristen Stewart, also walked the red carpet at Cannes Film Festival, whilst her other half carried out Cannes Jury Panel duties.
