Salma Hayek, a true icon of the entertainment industry, was born on September 2, 1966 and hails from Veracruz, Mexico. With a career that spans over three decades, this actress and filmmaker is renowned for her captivating dramatic abilities and drop-dead gorgeous looks. Salma made her mark on the small screen with her starring role in the telenovela Teresa in the late 80s and early 90s, before dominating the big screen with memorable performances in films like Desperado (1995), From Dusk till Dawn (1996), Wild Wild West (1999), and Dogma (1999).

But it was her role as Frida Kahlo in the 2002 biopic Frida that solidified Salma as a dramatic powerhouse and earned her an Academy Award nomination. Her star continued to rise with hit films such as Grown Ups (2010), Puss in Boots (2011), The Hitman’s Bodyguard (2017), and Marvel’s Eternals (2021). Salma’s versatility shines in her performances in Tale of Tales (2015), Beatriz at Dinner (2017), and House of Gucci (2021), showcasing her mastery of the craft.

Beyond her acting skills, Salma is also a fashion icon in her own right, always killing it with her style choices. She has a flair for the dramatic and isn’t afraid to embrace her curves, often opting for bold hues and luxurious fabrics like velvet and silk.

At the 28th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards in Santa Monica on Feb. 27, 2022, Salma slayed with her stunning ensemble. Dressed in a silky pink Gucci gown, with a bust-gathering silhouette that flowed effortlessly to the floor, Salma accessorized with sheer polka dot gloves, diamonds galore, and a high ponytail that showcased her smoky eyeshadow.
