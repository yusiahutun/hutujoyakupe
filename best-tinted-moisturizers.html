Why use tinted moisturizers for mature skin

When it comes to finding the best tinted moisturizers for mature skin, there are two key things to keep in mind: a lightweight formula and skincare ingredients. One of the sole purposes of a tinted moisturizer is to eliminate heavy layering of multiple products, such as foundation on top of primer on top of moisturizer, so the more lightweight, the better.

“A heavier product that is more of a foundation can oftentimes be too heavy for mature skin, sitting in fine lines and wrinkles,” says celebrity makeup artist Sarah Egan.

How tinted moisturizers help aging skin concerns

Read more: The Best Tinted Moisturizers For Mature Skin, According to Celebrity Makeup Artists

As far as skincare is concerned, the best tinted moisturizers should do exactly what the name suggests: moisturize. “This is so important, since as we age as skin loses moisture,” explains Egan.

Also key: As we age, we often notice our skin starting to form wrinkles, fine lines, blemishes and dark spots. And while these are all common, it’s still helpful to find a product to conceal these imperfections to make you feel more confident.

“A good tinted moisturizer will also help even the tone of your skin and reduce the appearance of redness. It will feel lightweight on the skin and won’t settle into fine lines and wrinkles,” says Egan. “It will also help mask imperfections, like sun spots, and minimize the appearance of pores.”

“Look for antioxidants in a tinted moisturizer as they will help improve skin texture and appearance from underneath the surface of the skin,” she says. To find them, look for tinted moisturizers with Vitamin A which has antioxidant properties and can help improve the appearance of fine lines and vitamin E which is anti-inflammatory that calms redness and irritation and adds moisture to the skin.
