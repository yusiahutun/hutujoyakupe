Oscar Isaac is a Guatemalan-born American actor and musician. Oscar Isaac is recognized for his versatility and has been credited with breaking stereotypes about Latino characters in Hollywood.

Early Life

Isaac was born Oscar Isaac Hernandez Estrada in Guatemala, USA, on March 9, 1979. He is 44 years old. His parents are a Guatemalan mother named Maria Eugenia, and a Cuban father named Oscar Gonzalo Hernandez-Cano who is a pulmonologist by profession. They together raised their son in Miami, Florida.

He has an older sister, a climate scientist named Nicole, and a younger brother, a journalist named Mike. Talking about his educational background, this actor attended the private grade school Westminster Christian School located in southern Florida. Moreover, he later attended and graduated from the Juilliard School in the year 2005.

Career

Before becoming an actor, he played lead guitar and sang vocals in his band named the Blinking Underdogs. Isaac was named the best actor of his generation in 2017 by Vanity Fair and one of the 25 greatest actors of the 21st century in 2020 by The New York Times.

His accolades include a National Board of Review Award, a Golden Globe Award, and a nomination for a Primetime Emmy Award. Moreover, he was featured on Time’s list of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2016. When he was an infant, he migrated with his family to the United States.

As a teenager, this actor joined a punk band, acted in plays, and also made his film debut in a minor role. Furthermore, he was a character actor in films for much of the 2000s. However, his first major role was that of Joseph in the biblical drama titled The Nativity Story (2006).

Also, he won an AACTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role for portraying East Timorese political leader Jose Ramos-Horta in the Australian film titled Balibo which was released in 2009. In addition, he became a global star with the role of Poe Dameron in the Star Wars sequel trilogy.

More about the career

After gaining popularity for playing supporting parts in Robin Hood and Drive, he had his breakthrough with the eponymous role of a singer in the musical drama known as Inside Llewyn Davis. This musical drama earned him a Golden Globe nomination. This amazing star starred in the historical drama named Operation Finale.

His career improved with leading roles in the crime drama A Most Violent Year, the thriller Ex Machina, and the superhero film X-Men: Apocalypse. However, this drama marked his first venture into production—the science fiction films Annihilation (2018) and Dune (2021), and the crime drama The Card Counter (2021).

On TV, he was the lead in three miniseries and they are Show Me a Hero, for which he earned a Golden Globe Award, Scenes from a Marriage, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe’s Moon Knight. Moreover, his stage work includes title roles in Romeo and Juliet (2007) and Hamlet (2017).
