Country Living Vintage Home: Fifth edition out now

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  251 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Country Living’s latest bookazine, the fifth edition of Vintage Home, is now on sale.

Inside, we highlight four different and distinctive looks that can bring vintage style into your interiors and garden – folk, modern retro, simply salvaged and English eccentric – all lending themselves perfectly to a country home.

What's inside Vintage Home?

Focusing on the looks one at a time and highlighting the charm and characteristics, each decorating section shows how you can incorporate furniture, furnishings and accessories into your home, either as a style by itself or beautifully blended with others.

We also look at popular collectibles that fit well within each section, such as Homemaker ceramics and vintage radios to Tolix chairs and glass demijohns, revealing their history and rise to popularity, and how you can find them to add to your own collection.

Plus, we meet ten shopholders from around the UK, including Nick Jenkins of Proper Old and the Jefferson-Halletts at Fir Tree House Vintage – all of whom have opened up fascinating and thriving emporiums.

You'll also find...

• A collection of inspiring houses showing how the owners have seamlessly incorporated elegant and striking vintage pieces

• A fascinating selection of features called Collecting & Curating about the essential vintage items, their background and how to style them

• A comprehensive sourcebook, including markets, fairs and online sellers, to help you track down your own treasures

How to buy Vintage Home

To order your copy at £14.99, visit hearstmagazines.co.uk/cl-specials or find it in selected retailers.
