Emma Stone Husband, Who's She Married To After Andrew Garfield Split?

 Posted on June 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Dave McCary (2017 - Present)

Emma Stone and her husband, Dave McCary, reportedly first met in December 2016 when the actress returned to host Saturday Night Live. A former SNL writer, McCary penned the “Wells for Boy” sketch, which Stone starred in. Rumors started swirling that the pair were an item in October 2017. A source close to Stone and McCary confirmed to People that they were dating for at least three months.

In January 2019, Stone and McCary stepped out for a public date night when they attended a Los Angeles Clippers game together. Over the next few months, they attended the SAG Awards and 2019 Oscars, where Stone was nominated for Best Supporting Actress. By April 2019, the actress was photographed wearing what seemed to be a diamond ring, but nothing was officially just yet. Finally, the couple confirmed their engagement in December 2014.

McCary shared a (now-deleted) photo on Instagram announcing their plans to wed. The photo depicted himself and Stone smiling as she held her pearl engagement ring, designed by jeweler Yoshinobu Kataoka, facing the camera. Unfortunately, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Page Six reported that the pair postponed their wedding in March 2020.

In September 2020, a source confirmed to People that McCary and Stone had secretly tied the knot earlier in the year. (Stone and McCary were pictured walking around L.A. wearing matching gold bands in the months prior.) The Poor Things star also appeared to be wearing a wedding ring during a video interview with Reese Witherspoon in May 2020.

One year later, the couple welcomed their daughter, Louise Jean, in March 2021. According to Vanity Fair, Stone’s daughter was named after her grandmother, Jean Louise. Stone and her mother, Krista Stone, also reportedly share the name Jean. “It’s very, very exciting,” Stone said about becoming a first-time mom during a video interview to promote her 2021 film Cruella. “I feel lucky with what lockdown has been but it has been an insane year.”
