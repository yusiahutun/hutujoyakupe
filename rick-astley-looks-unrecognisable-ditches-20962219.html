Rick Astley looks unrecognisable as he ditches red hair in transformation

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Rick Astley has ditched his iconic ginger locks for a darker look, as flaunted on today's Lorraine Kelly show.

Three decades after he skyrocketed to the top of the charts with his 1987 hit Never Gonna Give You Up, the singer looks as youthful as ever.

The 53-year-old chatted with host Lorraine Kelly about the release of his new record The Best of Me, featuring modern versions of his classic hit.

Just back from his tour with 90s boyband Take That, the pop sensation made it clear his hit single has stood the test of time.

He admitted: "I have a lot more fun now than I did back in the day.

"You do let go of things as you get older in a positive way."
