Prince Harry and William had 'fun night' partying with Paris Hilton at '$400-dollar-a-night hotel'

 Posted on June 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  602 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Paris Hilton has revealed that she once partied the night away with Prince William and Prince Harry.

During an appearance on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, Paris Hilton opened up about partying with royalty, and even revealed whether or not there was any flirting.

Paris Hilton says she, Prince William, and Prince Harry had ‘a fun night’ out

Andy asked Paris and her mom, Kathy, who joined her to promote the second season of Peacock’s reality series Paris In Love, about whether the pair had met any member of the royal family.

“Harry or William, have you met either of them?” Andy quizzed.

“I’ve met both of them,” replied Paris Hilton replied, confirming that she had met Prince William and Harry, with Kathy saying that she had “seen a picture of it.”

Paris Hilton said that she had partied with not only William and Prince Harry but also pop star Christina Aguilera at the London Hilton in Park Lane. The lavish hotel has rooms and suites on offer from $395 (£313) and can be as high as $830 or £631.

Andy asked the socialite whether there had been any “flirty energy” between herself and either of the brothers. She replied that there hadn’t been. She did say, however, that “we had a fun night.”

“It was me, Christina Aguilera, and the boys and a group of people. It was a fun night!” 42-year-old continued.

An encounter with Princess Diana

Kathy, 64, said that while she hadn’t met William or Harry like her daughter had, she’d had an encounter with their mom, the late Princess Diana.

Although the reality star declined, she was “buddies” with any of the royal family, as Andy had phrased it in his query.

She explained: “I did meet Princess Diana at the Met Ball years ago. And I have met King Charles and Camilla several times at Buckingham Palace.”

Paris speaks out against trolls over her son

It comes after Paris addressed trolls’ mean comments, who targeted her baby son Phoenix over the size of his head. She called out the negative response, saying her child is “her world.”

Paris took to her Instagram Stories: “This hurts my heart more deeply than words can describe. I’ve worked hard to cultivate an environment that is all about love, respect, and acceptance, and I expect the same in return.

“If I don’t post my baby, people assume I’m not a great mother, and if I do post him, there are some people who are cruel and hateful. I’m a proud working mom and my baby is perfectly healthy, adorable, and angelic.

“I’ve dreamt of being a mom for as long as I can remember. Phoenix is my world and has been the biggest blessing of my life. Every day with him is a reminder of what truly matters. It’s hard to fathom that there are people in the world who would target such innocence.”

The music star continued: “I hope that people can treat one another with more kindness and empathy.”

Later, she penned “of course he has been to a doctor” in comments under a TikTok that defended her son and asked: “What is wrong with this world?”

The celebrity wrote: “There are some sick people in this world ☹️ My angel is perfectly healthy. And yes, of course, he has been to a doctor, he just has a large brain.”
