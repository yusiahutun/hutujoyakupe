She certainly doesn't appear to be interested in the high end boutiques of Rodeo Drive.

Instead, Amber Heard - girlfriend of eccentric dresser Johnny Depp - seeks out a more unique and individual look, and to be fair, an altogether cheaper one.

The 27-year-old was spotted east of the famous shopping street at an eclectic flea market in West Hollywood which is held every Sunday.

Retail therapy: Amber Heard hit up the Melrose Trading Post flea market on Sunday after returning from a trip with Johnny Depp

The Melrose Trading Post market is crammed with thrifty goodies and takes place on the corner of Fairfax and the famous Melrose Avenue.

Amber already looked the part in a diaphanous white skirt and a matching lace camisole that she paired with a cream hat and simple thong sandals.

She's just returned from Moscow where she made a rare public appearance with boyfriend Johnny Depp, 50.

Following his attendance at the premiere of The Lone Ranger on Thursday night, the father-of-two met up with Amber for dinner at one of the city's top restaurants.

Radiant: Amber looked stunning in her all white outfit

While Johnny was still in his checked suit from the screening, Amber showed off her slim figure in a clingy satin burgundy dress.

Despite the presence of photographers, the couple appeared happy to be seen holding hands.
