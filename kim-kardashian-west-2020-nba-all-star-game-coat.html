Kim Kardashian West Wears a Puffer Coat at the NBA All-Star Game

 Posted on February 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  241 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kim Kardashian West may be busy promoting her many business endeavors, but that doesn’t mean she doesn’t save time for a little R&R. The media mogul spent last weekend celebrating Valentine’s Day with hubby Kanye West, hopping from Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, to Chicago in a matter of days.

Her first stop took her to a room with an oceanfront view, captioning on Snapchat how she was taking in a “little slice of heaven for Valentine’s Day.” Far away from the gaze of photographers, Kardashian West seemingly enjoyed being secluded from the circus that surrounds her daily. Her next stop, however, landed her right back in the middle of it.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

On Sunday, the couple was seated courtside at the 2020 NBA All-Star Game in her husband’s hometown of Chicago (and her daughter’s namesake), where she packed the heat in a puffer coat by Rick Owens. The bronze topper featured a pronounced collar, mimicking a shawl. She paired it with matching leather pants and beige heeled boots by Yeezy.

It was a look that adhered to the vibe of her shapewear line, SKIMS, and beauty brand, KKW Beauty. Both companies follow a minimalist, pared-down aesthetic that celebrates varying skin tones. And every bit the shrewd business woman she is, Kardashian West made sure to promote the image of her companies with an on-point ensemble, knowing full well that all eyes would be on her.
