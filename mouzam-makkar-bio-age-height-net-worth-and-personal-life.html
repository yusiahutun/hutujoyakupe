Mouzam Makkar Career

Before pursuing acting full time, Makkar served in investment banking and private equity. While working in Investment Banking, she wrote a monthly column for Window & Door Magazine titled “Windows on Wall Street.”

Later on, she quit her job and stepped in the acting industry making her debut movies Disgruntled as Tess Thompson in 2008.

After a few small roles in movie and TV show, she landed on the TV series, The Vampire Diaries where she played the role of Alex St. Jones in 2016. That same year, she portrayed the character Jessica in 2016 hit shows The Exorcist.  The following year, she appeared in 1 episode of Chicago Justice as Kalila Rafiq.
