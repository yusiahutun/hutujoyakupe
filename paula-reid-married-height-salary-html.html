Paula Reid is a Washington-based news correspondent for CBS. She covers the news related to the Justice Department, White House, and legal affairs.

She had initiated as a production secretary in New York when she first joined the network in 2010. After working for a year or so, she became a digital journalist for the area.

In the year 2014, she shifted to the branch of Washington, DC to start her career as a reporter. Paula's new job tasked her with covering news about significant cases and legal affairs. With her hard work and patience, Paula was succesful to achieve the title of correspondent in April 2018.

Within a year - in April of 2019 - Paula was promoted to the role of White House correspondent.

Her reporting got highlighted in many broadcasting platforms, including the CBS News' 24/7, CBS Evening News, CBS This Morning, Face the Nation, CBSN, and CBS News Radio.
