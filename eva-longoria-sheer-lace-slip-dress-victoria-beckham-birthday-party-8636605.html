See Eva Longoria's Sheer Lace Slip Dress

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Last night, Victoria Beckham celebrated her 50th birthday with a blowout bash in London, where style, star power, and the Spice Girls all came together for one unforgettable evening. And bringing two out of the three was Beckham's good friend, Eva Longoria, who honored the birthday girl with a look that was undoubtedly Posh-approved.  

Spotted arriving at the private members club Oswald's in the Mayfair district, Longoria wore a slinky black slip that toed the line between simple and sexy. The maxi-length dress effortlessly skimmed over her curves and featured an asymmetrical sheer lace panel that spanned from the top of her bust down to her torso. When she turned around, the see-through detailing continued from behind while also revealing a backless design. 

Longoria, who was joined by her husband José Bastón at the party, accessorized with a black leather clutch with chain-link handle, diamond stud earrings, and towering platform heels. She styled her hair sleek and straight with a middle part and tucked behind her ears, while a glossy nude lip and smoky eyes provided the finishing touches to her glam. 

Beckham and Longoria go way back. They first met in 2007 when Victoria and her husband, David, moved to Los Angeles for his soccer career, and the two have been "inseparable" ever since. "It wasn't until they moved to Los Angeles, when David started playing for LA Galaxy, that I did start to go to a lot of those games with Victoria," Longoria told Ross King on ITV's Lorraine back in June 2023. "We were inseparable. I mean, she's the funniest human-being — the loveliest, kindest, most loyal friend you could ever ask for."

They are so close, in fact, that Longoria is even considered an honorary Spice Girl. "I am secretly the fifth, Spicy Spice," she teased.
